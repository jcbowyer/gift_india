ABOUT DRASHTI EYE HOSPITAL

DRASHTI Eye Hospital is a well-equipped eye hospital with state of the art equipment which will take care of all your eye needs. We have practical experience in the determination of retinal eye diseases and serve medications for cataract, glaucoma, cornea, uveitis, oculoplastics, neuro-opthalmology, pediatric ophthalmology, refractive eye surgery and many more.
