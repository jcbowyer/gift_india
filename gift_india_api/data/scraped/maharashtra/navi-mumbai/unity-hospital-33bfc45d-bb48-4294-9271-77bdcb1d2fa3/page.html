About us

Welcome to Unity Hospital, Aundh, Pune – a trusted center for Orthopedics, Joint Replacement, Infertility, and Gynaec Endoscopy. Our expert team, led by Dr. Amit Kale Best Orthopedic and Joint Replacement Surgeon in Aundh, Pune and Dr. Preeti A. Kale Best Infertility Specialist in Aundh, Pune and Gynaec Endoscopist, is committed to delivering personalized and comprehensive healthcare tailored to each patient’s unique needs. With years of clinical expertise, we specialize in Knee and Shoulder Replacement Surgery, Partial Knee Replacement, Revision Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, Ligament Repair Surgery, Meniscus Tear Treatment, Sports Injury Care, Spine Surgery, Fracture and Trauma Management, Arthritis Treatment, Osteoporosis Treatment, and Pediatric Orthopedic Care. Dr. Preeti A. Kale also provides advanced women’s health services, including PCOD and PCOS treatment, Endometriosis management, Ovulation study, Ovarian cyst treatment, Low AMH therapy, PRP treatment, Thin endometrium care, Endometrial polyp removal, PID treatment, and Endometritis management—ensuring holistic care for reproductive and hormonal health.
 
At Unity Hospital in Aundh, Pune, we combine medical excellence with compassionate care under one roof. Our facility offers modern diagnostic and therapeutic technologies such as Robotic Knee Replacement, Hip Replacement Surgery, ACL Reconstruction, Nerve Root and Facetal Blocks, Regenerative Joint Preservation, Physiotherapy, and Digital X-rays, ensuring complete and convenient treatment for our patients. Whether you are seeking advanced orthopedic care or expert fertility and gynecological treatment, Unity Hospital provides comprehensive solutions backed by experience, innovation, and trust. Explore our website to know more about our facilities, access reliable health information, and book a secure online consultation. We look forward to helping you achieve better health and improved quality of life with expert care from Dr. Amit Kale and Dr. Preeti A. Kale in Aundh, Pune.
