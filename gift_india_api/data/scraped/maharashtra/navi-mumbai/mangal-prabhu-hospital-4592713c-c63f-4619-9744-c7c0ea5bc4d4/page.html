      FAQ
      1. What is a multispeciality hospital?

      Multispecialty hospitals provide comprehensive healthcare services across a variety of medical specialties. A range of medical specialties and healthcare professionals are on staff at these hospitals. Multispecialty hospitals diagnose, treat, and manage a wide range of medical conditions and illnesses.

      2. What is the difference between super-specialty and multispeciality hospitals?

      The difference between a multispecialty hospital and a super-specialty hospital is that super-specialists specialize in one specific area of medicine or surgery, while multispecialty hospitals cover a variety of medical specialties.

      A leading multispecialty hospital in Juinagar, Navi Mumbai, the Mangal Prabhu Hospital is known for its advanced medical treatments.
