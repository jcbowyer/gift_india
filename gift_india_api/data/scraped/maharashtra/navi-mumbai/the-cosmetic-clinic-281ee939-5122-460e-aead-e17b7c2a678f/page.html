Our primary services – Dental Clinic in Seawoods Navi Mumbai

Root canal treatment

Root canal treatment, or RCT, is a dental procedure that provides immediate relief from tooth pain caused by caries. It is an effective solution to remove the infected portion of your tooth and save it from extraction. The number of sittings required varies from case to case. RCT followed by a tooth cap is a good way to save your natural tooth. Root canal treatment is a painless procedure done under local anesthesia. 

Tooth extraction 

Tooth extraction is the painless tooth removal of a tooth from its socket under local anesthesia. It is safe and avoids damage to the surrounding bone and soft tissues. Wisdom teeth extraction involves minor surgery that can be done under local anesthesia. 

Dental crowns

Dental crowns or tooth caps are prostheses that support the tooth from all sides after root canal treatment. They help restore the structural durability of the remaining natural tooth. Various dental crowns are available, such as PFM, all-ceramic, and Emax crowns.  

Dental bridges

Dental bridges effectively replace missing teeth by utilizing support from adjacent teeth. They are fixed replacements for missing teeth. Just like dental crowns, we have a variety of dental bridges available depending on the material used. 

Dental implants

Dental implants are the latest method for replacing missing teeth. They involve directly placing a titanium post into the jawbone and do not interfere with adjacent teeth. The procedure is painless and done under local anesthesia. Implants act as artificial roots over which tooth caps are planned after a few months. 

Dental veneers

Dental veneers are thin shells of porcelain or composite material designed to attach to the front surface of the teeth. They are the most versatile way to enhance your smile. Dental veneers are used in cases of intrinsic staining, minor chipped teeth, mild misalignments, and small gaps between the teeth. 

Tooth colored fillings

Dental fillings restore the decayed portion of teeth when the nerves and vessels are not involved. Tooth-colored fillings are the latest method to restore teeth without compromising their aesthetics. They are a durable and long-lasting solution to tooth damage. When done on time, they prevent the spread of tooth damage, reducing the need for RCT in the future. 

Gums treatment

Gum reshaping or recontouring is a standard treatment done under local anesthesia. Gingivectomy and gingivoplasty help improve the gum contour. Gum surgeries are also indicated when crown lengthening is preferred before tooth caps are planned. 

Teeth cleaning

Professional teeth cleaning services are suggested for each individual every 3-4 months. These services help remove plaque and tartar deposits from the teeth’ surface. Scaling or professional cleaning helps promote good gum health.  

Teeth whitening

Teeth whitening under a dentist’s supervision is a safer and more effective way to improve your smile. It is better than all the do-it-yourself teeth whitening hacks and products. Professional whitening requires one sitting to provide uniform and better results. 

Kids dental treatment

Kids’ dentistry is an essential part of dental treatments done by pediatric dentists. Like any other medical treatment, it requires special attention and care. Our in-house specialist team helps us provide all kinds of kids’ dental treatments under one roof, making us one of the best Dental Clinic in Seawoods Navi Mumbai.

Dentures 

Dentures are removable prostheses used to replace missing teeth. They are indicated in cases where jawbone health does not allow for fixed prostheses. Depending on the missing teeth, dentures can be complete or partial. We are one of the few Dental Clinic in Seawoods Navi Mumbai ,providing specialized care for old patients. 

Dental Braces

Traditional braces are the oldest way of correcting misaligned teeth. They consist of brackets and wire systems that exert mild pressure on the teeth to move them into the desired location. Braces can be of various types, such as metal braces, ceramic braces, and lingual braces. 

Clear Aligners

Aligners are a clear set of transparent plates that fit onto your teeth and work similarly to braces. Unlike traditional braces, clear aligners are removable and aesthetic. We are one of the few Dental Clinic in Seawoods Navi Mumbai providing different varieties of clear aligners. 

Smile Makeover

Smile design or makeover is a sequential set of treatments to enhance your smile. It can include a couple or more aesthetic treatments done to help improve your overall smile. Smile designing is a specialized process done in a very few Dental Clinic in Seawoods Navi Mumbai.
