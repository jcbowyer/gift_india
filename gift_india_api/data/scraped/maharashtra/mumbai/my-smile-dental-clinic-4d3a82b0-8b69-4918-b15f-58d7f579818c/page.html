Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a dental filling cost in Mumbai?

The cost of a dental filling in Mumbai depends on the material used and the cavity size. Tooth-colored composite fillings are generally more expensive than silver ones. A quick consultation can help you understand the best option and estimated charges.

2. How much does a root canal cost in Mumbai?

Root canal costs can vary based on the tooth involved, the complexity of the case, and the dentist’s expertise. Front teeth are usually simpler and less expensive than molars. The overall cost may also include crown placement.

3. Is teeth whitening safe and effective?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is both safe and effective when done by a qualified dentist. It helps remove stains and brightens your smile in just one session. Results may vary depending on lifestyle and oral habits.

4. How often should I visit a dentist?

It’s recommended to visit your dentist every 6 months for a routine checkup. Regular visits help detect problems early and keep your teeth healthy. Even if you’re not in pain, preventive care is essential.

5. What are the signs of a dental cavity?

Common signs include tooth sensitivity, visible holes, chewing pain, or dark spots. If untreated, cavities can lead to infections or tooth loss. Early treatment with fillings can prevent further damage.
