    Frequently Asked Questions On Our Multispeciality Hospital in Mumbai

    Does Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital offer cashless hospitalization?

    Yes, Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital is a leading cashless hospital in Goregaon, Mumbai. We have partnerships with several top-tier health insurance providers, ensuring seamless and hassle-free cashless hospitalization for our patients.

    What are the specialties of Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital?

    Our specialties include laparoscopic surgery, orthopedic surgery, cancer surgery, laser kidney stone surgery, laser proctology, ENT surgery, and gynecological surgery. We also offer specialized quarantine ICU wards for critical care patients.

    How can I book an appointment at Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital in Mumbai?

    You can book an appointment by visiting our website at Kapadia Hospital or by calling our hospital's reception desk. Online appointment booking options are available for your convenience.

    Does Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital have ICU facilities?

    Yes, Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital is equipped with specialized ICU facilities, including quarantine ICU wards, to provide critical care to patients in need.

    Does Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital offer emergency services?

    Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services. Our emergency department is equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies, with experienced doctors and advanced medical technology on standby.

    Does Kapadia Multispeciality Hospital offer second opinions?

    Yes, we offer second opinions for patients seeking additional consultation regarding their diagnosis or treatment plan. Our team of specialists is available to provide expert guidance and ensure you receive the best possible care.
