Dr. Apratim Goel

MBBS, MD – Skin & VD, DNB – Dermatology & Venereology, Fellow of Academy of General Education (FAGE). Renowned Dermatologist with 17+ Years Experience. Dr. Apratim Goel, the face behind Cutis is a compassionate clinician, who brings along experience, proficiency and that gentle care & organized mind-set of a woman, which gets internalised in the whole setup. As a CEO/Medical Director of Cutis Skin Studio, she is extremely passionate about aesthetic and cosmetic medicine and is constantly striving to offer state of the art personalized treatment to her patients. She is also skilled in the field of lasers and non-surgical cosmetic treatments. She has a voracious appetite for knowledge and keeps herself updated on the new technologies in the field of skincare by attending extensive training courses on various lasers, thread lifts, mesotherapy, fillers and other cosmetic procedures at prestigious institutions in the US, Paris, Bangkok, Singapore and Italy.
