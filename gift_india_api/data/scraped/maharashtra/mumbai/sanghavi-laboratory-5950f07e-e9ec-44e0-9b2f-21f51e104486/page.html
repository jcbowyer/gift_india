    Our Story

    ×

    Sanghavi Laboratory was established in 1974 by Shri M. N. Sanghavi primarily to give quick and accurate reports to patients admitted in adjacent Kamdar Nursing home. With the passage of time after 44 years of service to patients, we have established ourselves as a laboratory maintaining highest standards of quality in blood sample testing. Friendly and personal attention to each and every patient. Blood collection of infants and children is done exclusively by our highly skilled staff. All patients are attended personally by our senior staff
