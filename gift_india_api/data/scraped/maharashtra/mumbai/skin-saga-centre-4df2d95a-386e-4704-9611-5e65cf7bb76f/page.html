Welcome to Skin Saga

Located in the heart of Mumbai, Skin Saga Centre for Dermatology is one of the best skin clinics offering a one-stop solution to all issues related to skin, hair and aesthetics. 

Founded by internationally trained, highly recommended, award winning dermatologists - Dr Madhulika Mhatre MD and Dr Aseem Sharma MD, at Skin saga Centre for Dermatology, you can be rest assured to receive well- recommended, and safe and effective treatment options.

Our dermatologists approach each case as per the patient’s individual requirements so as to tailor a customized treatment regimen that would best suit them, giving them superior results. Perhaps, this is the secret behind the high satisfaction levels in all our patients.
