All FAQs

Who is the best ophthalmologist in Marine Lines, Mumbai?

Dr Dipti Shah at Sadhana Eye Care is a leading ophthalmologist in Marine Lines with 28+ years of experience, rated 4.7 on Google and 98% recommended on Practo. She specialises in cataract and LASIK surgery, seeing every patient personally at the clinic on Princess Street.

How long does cataract surgery take?

Cataract surgery by phacoemulsification typically takes 3–5 minutes per eye. It is a blade-free, day-care procedure done under anaesthetic eye drops, with no injection or stitches in routine cases. Most patients go home the same day and resume light activities within a day or two.

Am I a good candidate for LASIK?

LASIK suitability depends on your spectacle power, corneal thickness and overall eye health. A detailed LASIK screening confirms whether your eyes are suitable. At Sadhana Eye Care, Dr Dipti Shah will tell you honestly if LASIK is right for you, or recommend a safer alternative.
