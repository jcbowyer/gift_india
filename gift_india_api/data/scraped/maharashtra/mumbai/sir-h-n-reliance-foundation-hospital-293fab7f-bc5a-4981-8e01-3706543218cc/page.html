Success Stories

As a lawyer, terms like "heart attack" and "angioplasty" were not part of my daily life—until one hit me, completely out of the blue. Beyond the physical toll, the mental strain and anxiety were overwhelming.

Thankfully, Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital became my guiding light during this challenging time. From the swift actions of Dr. Rishikesh in the ER to Dr. Manoj Mushru and his team's expertise during my procedure, the care was exceptional. They kept me informed every step of the way, easing my anxiety.

Post-surgery, Dr. Aashish Contractor's informed advice and support, along with Dr. Disha Pujari and Dr. Sanika Singh’s relentless efforts during my recovery, helped me regain my strength. Their dedication and encouragement made all the difference.

This journey was also made bearable by the unwavering support of my wife, a physiotherapist at the hospital, whose strength became my anchor.

Today, I stand stronger—thanks to the incredible team at H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital. They are truly a trusted partner in heart health.

~Aditya Raut

During the ninth month of my pregnancy, I began experiencing severe breathing issues. None of the hospitals were willing to take my case, and I was losing hope as my condition worsened.

At 3:00 a.m., we rushed to Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital based on a recommendation, and it was the best decision we made. The care and treatment I received not only restored my hope but also ensured a normal delivery of my daughter.

Under the expert guidance of Dr. Talha Meeran, my heart's pumping capacity improved from 30% to 50%. Even now, as I continue my treatment, Dr. Meeran and his team provide exceptional care, patiently addressing all my queries and guiding me through every step.

I am deeply grateful to Dr. Talha Meeran, Dr. Himangi, and the entire team at Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital for their unwavering support and efforts.

~Aparna Borade

Two years ago, my life took an unexpected turn when I was told I needed a heart transplant due to a sudden and severe medical condition. Under the expert care of Dr. Anvay Mulay and his team at Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital, I began my journey of hope.

After nine months of waiting, I received the greatest gift—a new heart from an anonymous donor. Today, over a year post-transplant, I am living life to the fullest, pursuing my passions like motorcycle riding and traveling—dreams I thought I had lost forever.

I am profoundly grateful to the anonymous donor who gave me a second chance and to Dr. Mulay and the entire team, including the cardiac rehabilitation experts, who guided me through this journey. Thanks to them, I am embracing every moment of life with renewed joy and gratitude.

~Pt. Saurabh Amlani

At 53, I suffered a severe heart attack with complications that brought me close to losing hope. After surgery, I faced low blood pressure and a reduced heart pumping capacity of 32%. But I refused to give up.

Since March 2017, I’ve been undergoing cardiac rehab at Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital under Dr. Aashish Contractor and practicing yoga with Ms. Nityatara. With over 600 rehab sessions and 400 yoga sessions completed, I can confidently say I’m living a near-normal life within my parameters.

My message to everyone: Take care of your heart before it’s too late, and if you face challenges, embrace regular exercise like cardiac rehab and yoga. Your heart is powerful yet sensitive—treat it with care.

~Dhiren Dalal

At 63, having survived five heart attacks, two angioplasties, and a CABG, I was struggling with low confidence and a lack of spirit. On my doctor’s recommendation, I joined the Cardiac Rehab Cardiac Rehab Programme at Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital, and it has truly been life-changing.

For the past two years, the structured exercises and care provided here have greatly improved my well-being. The program has worked wonders for my recovery, and I couldn’t be more grateful.

~Dr. Bhavna Shah

They say you only live once, but I believe you live twice—when given a second chance. After undergoing CABG in January, I’m now back to running and living life to the fullest, thanks to Dr. Bhamre, Dr. Mashru, Dr. Contractor, and the incredible Rehab team at Sir H.N. Reliance Foundation Hospital.

Today, I run at a speed of 9, with no regrets about my heart attack. Life isn’t over after a heart attack; it’s a chance to begin again, stronger and on your own terms.

To anyone facing similar challenges, don’t despair, take action, embrace life, and live life happily—twice over!

~Pt. Kumar Gianaani
