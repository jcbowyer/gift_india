Our Mission

To make “Chavan Hospital” the most advanced, respected and
affordable medical institution of mumbai.

Our Aim

Our aim is to Provid the highest quality patient care at affordable rate. Provide all available and possible medical facilities charity to humanity by providing best medical treatment irrespective of caste and religion.

Our Vision

To be the hospital of choice for patients, physicians and employees by providing state-of the art medical care with compassion and dignity.

About us

“Chavan Hospital was set up in 1982 with a desire to help community around as there were no hospitals in the vicinity. Our main founder, Mrs. Parvatibai Chavan, wanted to ensure that we provide the best possible medical care using state-of- the-art technology to every single patient, irrespective of their social background or financial ability.

Last Thirty eight years we are proudly standing tall and everyday we try to give best of the medical services to all.

we strive every single day to live up to these goals. We strive to attract the best of the best doctors to provide best care. We strive to provide compassionate and well-trained nurses so that every patient receives the best possible care. We strive to install cutting- edge technology to help diagnose and cure the most complex and challenging illnesses.

We everyday put efforts to upgrade our hospital processes so that the patient experience the best, from the time they check in to the time they are discharged.

We are proud to be a charitable trust which makes it possible to provide free or highly subsidized care to the poorest and most needy.
