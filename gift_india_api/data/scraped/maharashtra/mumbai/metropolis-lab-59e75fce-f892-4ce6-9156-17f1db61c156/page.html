About Us

Metropolis Healthcare is a leading diagnostic service provider in India. We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to providing you with accurate and reliable diagnostic services. Our NABL and CAP-accredited pathology labs and diagnostic centres are equipped with the latest technology and manned by a certified team of 200 senior pathologists and over 2000 technicians.

At Metropolis Healthcare, we provide a wide range of diagnostic services ranging from complete health check-up packages for men, women, senior citizens, youth, & corporates to super-speciality tests like Oncology, Neurology, Gynaecology, Nephrology and many more. Our diagnostic services are trusted by leading doctors and hospitals, and we take pride in delivering quality reports.

We understand that visiting a diagnostic centre or pathology lab can be a daunting experience for many. That is why we offer the option of booking blood tests or expert health check-up packages online. You can either visit our nearest Metropolis Lab to give your blood sample or opt for blood collection at home. This way, you can get all the benefits of a diagnostic centre and pathology labs from the comfort of your home.

At Metropolis Healthcare, we are committed to providing you with a hassle-free and seamless experience. We believe that early detection and accurate diagnosis are critical for effective treatment, and we are here to help you achieve the same. So, book your appointment today and experience the difference with Metropolis Healthcare.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many labs and centres does Metropolis Healthcare have at present in India?

Metropolis Healthcare has 170+ advanced clinical labs with certified experts, offering a range of diagnostic services including oncology, neurology, and nephrology tests. Their PAN India presence in 220 cities and 12000+ patient touchpoints provide access to 4000+ tests and profiles. They are committed to delivering credible and timely diagnostic services.

What types of pathology tests does Metropolis Healthcare offer?

Metropolis Healthcare offers a range of diagnostic services including routine, semi-speciality and super-speciality tests like Oncology, Neurology, Gynecology, Nephrology, and complete health check-up packages for men, women, senior citizens, youths & corporates.

How do I prepare for my pathology test?

The preparation for a pathology test may vary depending on the type of test. You can find specific instructions for preparation on Metropolis Healthcares official website or can contact our customer care service for assistance.

How long does it take to get my test results back?

The turnaround time for test results may vary depending on the type of test. We provide estimated turnaround times for each test on our official website. You can also inquire about the expected turnaround time from the lab.

Can I request a copy of my pathology report?

Yes, you can request a copy of your pathology report by contacting Metropolis Healthcares customer care service, downloading it from the app or website or visiting the lab in person.

How do you ensure the accuracy of your test results?

Metropolis Healthcare ensures the accuracy of test results through a stringent quality control process that includes regular calibration of equipment, external and internal quality checks, and adherence to international standards.

How can I find out information regarding a particular lab/centre about Metropolis Healthcare?

To locate a specific lab/center of Metropolis Healthcare, visit our website and search for the location. You can find details such as the address, phone number, operating hours, and services offered. Contact customer care for further information.

What are the hours of operation for your lab?

The hours of operation for Metropolis Healthcare labs may vary depending on the location. You can check the hours of operation for a specific lab by contacting our customer care service.

How will I receive my test results?

You can receive your test results through Metropolis Healthcares online portal, app, email, WhatsApp or by visiting the lab in person.

Do I need to make an appointment to have a pathology test done?

You are advised to make an appointment to have a pathology test service done at home to avoid any inconvenience. However, you can walk in at your nearest lab without an appointment too.

Are your lab technicians certified and licensed?

Yes, Metropolis Healthcare employs a certified team of over 200 senior pathologists and over 2000 technicians who are trained to provide diagnostic services.
