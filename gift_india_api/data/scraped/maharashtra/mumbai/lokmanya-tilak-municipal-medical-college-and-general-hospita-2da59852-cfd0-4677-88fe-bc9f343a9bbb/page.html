Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College
and Lokmanya Tilak Municipal General Hospital , Sion Mumbai

Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College and General Hospital (LTMGH), Sion, is one of the most reputed medical colleges and research institutes in India, abiding by our motto “Service through Excellence.”

Established over five decades ago, the college has successfully completed 53 years of imparting quality undergraduate medical education and 40 years of providing postgraduate training in a wide range of degree and diploma courses under the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS), Nashik. The institution also offers super-specialty programs such as D.M. and M.Ch. to nurture advanced medical expertise.

The college was granted permanent affiliation by the University of Mumbai and is presently affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik. Over the years, it has grown into a center of excellence, combining education, healthcare, and research to serve society with compassion and innovation.
