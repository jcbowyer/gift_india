Special Care for Anxious People

Odontophobia or fear of dental pain is a very real thing. Sedation Dentistry is ideal for patients with dental anxiety, fear of pain, or those undergoing longer procedures, nitrous oxide helps you relax without losing consciousness.Our premium dental clinic offers sedation dentistry with nitrous oxide, commonly known as "laughing gas," to ensure a stress-free and comfortable dental experience.This safe and effective sedative is inhaled through a mask, offering quick relief from anxiety while keeping you calm and at ease. The effects wear off quickly, allowing you to resume normal activities soon after your appointment.Our flagship practice in Lokhandwala, Andheri, Mumbai is one of a few clinics that offers this highly underrated service in house.

Strong Safety Measures for Your Peace of Mind

Your safety is our top priority. To ensure the safety of all our patients and personnel, we follow strict infection control practices and adhere to worldwide hygiene standards. Our safety precautions include:

100% Sanitisation Protocols: Each treatment room and all dental equipment are properly sanitised before and after each usage. We utilise modern sterilisation processes to remove the possibility of contamination.

Use of PPE Kits: All of our dental professionals are outfitted with Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) to guarantee their safety during procedures.

Air Purification Systems: We use high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters to decrease airborne pollutants, resulting in a clean and healthy clinic atmosphere.

Regular Staff Training: Our crew receives periodic training to remain up to speed on the newest safety rules and infection control methods.
