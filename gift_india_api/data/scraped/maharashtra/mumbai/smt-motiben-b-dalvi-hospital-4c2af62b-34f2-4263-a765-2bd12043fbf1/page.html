Welcome to
Quality Healthcare at Affordable Price

Smt. Motiben Dalvi Hospital, is a recognized name in Patient Care. It was incepted in the year 1946. We are one of the well-known Private Hospitals in Grant Road. Backed with a vision to offer the best in patient care and equipped with technologically advanced healthcare facilities, we are one of the upcoming names in the healthcare industry. A team of well-trained medical staff, non-medical staff and experienced clinical technicians work round-the-clock to offer Medical Services. The Hospital has support of over 130 Consultants including Surgeons. The Hospital has facility of 57 beds. The Hospital has recently completed majorly renovation and refurbishment of its third floor which now has 7 bedded ICU and 6 Deluxe Rooms. This facility is comparable to other hospital in the vicinity
