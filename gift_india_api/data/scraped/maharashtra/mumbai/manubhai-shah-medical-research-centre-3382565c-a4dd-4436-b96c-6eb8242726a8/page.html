    Frequently Asked Questions

    What services are available at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre?

    Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre is a multi-specialty diagnostic and medical centre in Mumbai offering services such as CT scan, digital X-ray, pathology tests, sonography, cardiology consultation, orthopedic care, dermatology, physiotherapy, ENT services, dentistry, and general physician consultations.

    Where is Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre located?

    Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre is located in Kalachowki, Mumbai and serves patients from nearby areas including Lalbaug, Byculla, Parel, Mazgaon, Sewri, and South Mumbai.

    Does Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre provide diagnostic tests in Mumbai?

    Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre provides a wide range of diagnostic tests including CT scans, digital X-ray imaging, sonography, pathology lab tests, and health checkups using modern medical equipment.

    How can I book an appointment at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre?

    Patients can book an appointment at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre by calling the clinic directly or submitting an appointment request through the website contact form.

    Does Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre offer orthopedic consultation in Mumbai?

    Yes, Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre offers orthopedic consultation for patients experiencing joint pain, fractures, sports injuries, arthritis, and other musculoskeletal conditions.

    Is X-ray imaging available at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre?

    Yes, Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre provides digital X-ray services in Mumbai to help diagnose fractures, bone injuries, chest conditions, and other medical issues.

    What diagnostic facilities are available at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre?

    The centre provides several diagnostic facilities including pathology laboratory services, CT scan, sonography, 2D echo, digital X-ray, and other medical investigations to support accurate diagnosis.

    Why choose Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre for medical services in Mumbai?

    Patients choose Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre because of its experienced medical professionals, modern diagnostic equipment, accurate reports, and convenient location in Kalachowki, Mumbai.

    Does Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre provide physiotherapy services?

    Yes, Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre offers physiotherapy services to help patients recover from injuries, orthopedic conditions, and mobility issues.

    What medical specialists are available at Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre?

    Manubhai Shah Medical & Research Centre offers consultations with specialists in orthopedics, cardiology,  dermatology, ENT, Nephrology, Dentistry, General medicine, Ophthalmology Urology, Gastroenterology, Gynaecology, Pediatrics, Physiotherapy, Diabetology, Neurology, General Surgery, Respiratory, Radiology, Spine Surgery, Dietetics, Endocrinology and other healthcare services.
