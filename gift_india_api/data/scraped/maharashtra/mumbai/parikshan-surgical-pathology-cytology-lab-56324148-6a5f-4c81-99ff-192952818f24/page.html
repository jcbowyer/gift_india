About Dr. Gauri

Experienced Professional !

Dr. Gauri Chapolikar Vidolkar is an MD Pathologist with a gold medal from one of India's top colleges Kasturba Medical College (Mangalore), Manipal University. She completed her MBBS from LTMMC Sion Hospital, Mumbai.

She has worked extensively and exclusively in the field of Histopathology, Cytology, Immunohistochemistry with prime focus on Oncopathology. Prior to starting her own lab, she worked as a Consultant at Centre of Excellence for Histopathology, SRL Diagnostics. She is also accredited by NABL as an authorised signatory in histopathology.
