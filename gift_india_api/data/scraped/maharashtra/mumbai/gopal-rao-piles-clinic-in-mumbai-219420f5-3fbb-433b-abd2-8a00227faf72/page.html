Dr. Kota’s passion for surgery became evident during his internship, where he developed a keen interest in surgical procedures.

 

Professional Training and Experience:

Dr. Mukesh Kota received invaluable guidance and training from his father, Dr. S. M. Kota, who is an experienced practitioner in the field of Piles, Fissure, and Fistula. During his college breaks, Dr. Mukesh Kota actively participated in his father’s clinic, where he gained hands-on experience in wound dressing and administering fluids to patients. His compassionate and polite demeanor earned him a reputation for providing sound advice and personalized treatment recommendations.

 

Piles, Fissure, and Fistula Expertise:

With seven years of dedicated practice, Dr. Mukesh Kota has successfully treated a significant number of patients suffering from Piles, Fissure, and Fistula. Driven by a commitment to patient care, Dr. Kota ensures that each individual receives the utmost attention and comprehensive treatment for their condition.
