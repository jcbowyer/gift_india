About Our Center

Awarded Best Dentists in Mumbai

Dr Sankpals Dental Centre is dedicated to providing excellence in the field of Dentistry. With world-class dental facilities, cutting-edge technology, wonderful support staff & soothing environment in the Hiranandani Gardens, Powai area, our team is committed to making you smile everyday, in every way.

Combining Cosmetic Dentistry, Dental Implants, Orthodontics, Full mouth Rehab,Oral Surgery, Zoom Teeth Whitening & Dental Lasers, we’ve been tagged as the Best Dental Care providers in Mumbai. Our positive testimony from our patients & our best practices form a part of our innovation, culture and care that we follow at our centre.

Our general dentists & hygienists advise children & adults on how best to care for their teeth and gums to prevent dental problems, as well as fixing current ones. Cosmetic dentistry focuses on smile aesthetics with bespoke veneers, white fillings and crowns and teeth whitening to provide that extra sparkle. Our Smile Makeover in a Day is ideal for those short of time and seeking the perfect smile. We also treat patients with gingivitis (inflamed or bleeding gums) or periodontal disease (which affects the gums and underlying bone). Dental implants are the ideal solution after dental trauma and a permanent alternative to dentures, allowing patients to eat and smile with confidence.

About Dr. Rajendra Sankpal

Dr. Rajendra K Sankpal is a Dental Surgeon and an Oral Implantologist & he has over 26 yrs of clinical experience in the field of dentistry. Dr. R. K. Sankpal graduated from Government Dental College and Hospital Mumbai. He was an ex-assistant lecturer at Government Dental College, Mumbai before he started his private practice in 1993. He worked as assistant lecturer in the department of prosthetic dentistry at Government Dental College and Hospital Mumbai. Dr Sankpal has also worked as a clinical assistant at Government Dental College and Hospital Mumbai.

Dr Sankpal has always believed in upgrading his knowledge & techniques in the field of dentistry. Thus resulting in attending many national & international dental conferences. He is trained in computer guided implant dentistry at Leuven University (Belgium ), 3D digital dentistry-materialize dental (Belgium). He is also certified by the Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry in India & is the winner of “Scholastic Award” from Indian Dental Association Mumbai. Read more...
