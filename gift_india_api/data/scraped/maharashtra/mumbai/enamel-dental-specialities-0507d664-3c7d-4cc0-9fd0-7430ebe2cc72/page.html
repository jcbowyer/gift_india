Meet Our Experienced Dentists in Prabhadevi

Dr. Arnie Panchamia Kokate

Aesthetic & Cosmetic Dentistry (USA)

Dr. Arnie Panchamia Kokate believes that a beautiful smile can boost your confidence & bring positivity in life.

She specializes in Aesthetic & Cosmetic Dentistry & has completed her PG Diploma in Aesthetic Dentistry from UCLA, Los Angeles, USA.

She strives to achieve good oral health for her patients, along with giving them a perfect smile.

Dr. Aditya Kokate

Prosthodontist

Dr Aditya Kokate is Prosthodontist, specializing in crowns & veneers, full mouth rehabilitation and dental implants.

He has post – graduate diploma (P.G.D) in dental implantology from CIIOMFI, France.

He is a member of the Indian Prosthodontic Society (IPS) and the Indian Society of Oral Implantology (ISOI).

Dr. Sharad Kokate

Endodontist

Dr Sharad Kokate is an Endodontist with over 35 years of clinical & academic experience. He has been exclusively performing root canal treatments and has an expertise in handling complicated re-root canal cases with emphasis on “saving the natural tooth”.

He is the ex-DEAN of Y.M.T. Dental College & Hospital, where he was also the Head of Department and a guide to many postgraduate students.
