Dr. Dnyanesh Nikam

Director, Sushrut Ano-Rectal Hospital & Research Centre Nasik.

Dr. Dnyanesh Nikam is a well known doctor in Maharashtra for treating Ano-Rectal diseases like Piles, Fissures, Fistula. A Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery, Dr. Nikam has treated patients with para-surgical procedure, by using principles of Ayurveda (Indian science) & Modern science technology.
He founded Sushrut Ano-Rectal Hospital & Research Centre. The hospital also serves as private training centre for doctors in Ano-Rectal diseases.
