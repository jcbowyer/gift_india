About CareHabitat

CareHabitat offers a specialized cardiac, pulmonary, and geriatric rehabilitation program to help individuals improve their ability to function and increase their physical endurance, enhance their ability to perform daily living skills, reduce lifestyle-related risks, and increase knowledge of disease and prevention.

At Carehabitat, to help you achieve your health goals, we evaluate your physical capabilities and limitations and develop a rehabilitation program tailored specifically to your needs.
