Pilonidal sinus or cyst is regarded as the small hole or channel in the skin. The Pilonidal Sinus is the form of cyst which is filled with the pus, hairs, dirt, and debris may lead to severe pain.

Perianal Abscess

A perianal abscess is a localized collection of pus that forms near the anus due to infection. It typically occurs when a small gland located just inside the anus becomes blocked and infected. .

Sentinel Piles

"Sentinel piles," also known as "skin tags," are small flaps of skin located near the anal opening. They are not actual hemorrhoids or piles, but they can be related to other anorectal conditions, such as anal fissures.
