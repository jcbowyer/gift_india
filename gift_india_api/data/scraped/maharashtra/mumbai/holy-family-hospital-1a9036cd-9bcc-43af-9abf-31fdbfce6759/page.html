Legend of Holy Family Hospital:
The Legacy of Mother Giovanna

In the year 1978, Mother Giovanna arrived in Mumbai to take over the Holy Family Hospital which was handed over to the Ursuline Sisters of Mary Immaculate from the Medical Mission Sisters. Mother Giovanna was the pioneer and primary architect behind its phenomenal growth.
