Dr Dilip Rajpal

Laparoscopic Surgeon / General Surgeon

 +91-7039000696

Dr Dilip Rajpal is a Laparoscopic Surgeon and General Surgeon in Chembur, Mumbai and has an experience of 20+ years in these fields. Dr. Dilip Rajpal practices at Chirayu Healthcare Polyclinic & Diagnostics in Chembur, Mumbai, Godrej Memorial Hospital in Vikhroli, Mumbai and Apollo Spectra Hospital in Chembur, Mumbai. He completed MS,MAIS,FICS,Diploma in Laparoscopic Surgery,Fellow in Robotic & Adv Laparoscopic Colo-Rectal Surgery(Korea University) & BYL Nair Charitable Hospital in 1995, MS – General Surgery from Grant Medical College and Sir JJ Hospital, Mumbai in 1999 and FICS – General Surgery from International College Of Surgeon in 2008.

He is a member of Association of Surgeons of India (ASI), Association of Minimal Access Surgeons of India (AMASI), International College of Surgeons (ICS) and Fellow of advanced laparoscopic colo-rectal and robotic surgery, Korea University (ANAM Hospital). Some of the services provided by the doctor are SILS (Single incision laparoscopic Cholecystectomy surgery) for Gall bladder calculli (stone), Sils Appendectomy for Appendicitis, laparoscopic Hernia repair surgery for inguinal, ventral & incisional hernia, laser treatment of varicose veins (EVLA- Endovenous laser Ablation of varicose veins), painless piles surgery (MIPH-Stappler piles surgery, suture Rectopexy), complicated & recurrent fistula treatment for fistula ano, lateral internal sphincterotomy for fissure ano, abdominal pain treatment, etc.
