About Us

Dr Rustom F Ginwalla is a trained Board Certified Plastic and Aesthetic surgeon, with a special interest in the field of cosmetic surgery.

He completed his basic medical and surgical training at the Kasturba Medical College, Mangalore and his training in Plastic Surgery at Bombay Hospital of Medical Sciences, Mumbai. He has worked for six years at Bombay Hospital under the able guidance of senior plastic surgeons, gaining valuable insight and honing his surgical skills. Here, he has also gained a lot of experience in surgery of the hand. He has also completed a Fellowship of the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh (F.R.C.S.Ed.).
