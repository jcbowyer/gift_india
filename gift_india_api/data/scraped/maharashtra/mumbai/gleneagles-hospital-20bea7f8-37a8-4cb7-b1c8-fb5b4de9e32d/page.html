Faq
What specialties are available?

Gleneagles Hospital, Parel offers specialties like Cardiology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Hepatology, Nephrology, Critical Care, Surgery (including robotic and minimally invasive), Urology, Orthopaedics, Oncology, Transplants, and more, providing comprehensive care with expert doctors and advanced technology.

Is it a 24/7 emergency hospital?

Yes, Gleneagles Hospital operates a 24/7 emergency department with advanced life-support, trauma care, and critical care teams available round the clock for immediate medical attention and emergency interventions.

How to book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked via the hospital website or call at 022 6767 0101. Prior booking is recommended for timely consultation.

Does the hospital accept cashless insurance/TPA?

Yes, the hospital accepts cashless insurance and TPA services. Patients should confirm coverage with their provider beforehand to ensure smooth billing and admission.

Are advanced surgeries like robotic or minimally invasive procedures available?

Yes, Gleneagles offers advanced robotic and minimally invasive surgeries with expert surgeons and modern technology for faster recovery and minimal scarring.

Why choose Gleneagles Hospital, Mumbai?

Gleneagles offers expert doctors, advanced technology, multi-organ transplant expertise, including robotic and patient-focused care, making it a top choice for complex and routine treatments.

What amenities are available for inpatients?

Inpatients enjoy private rooms, ICU care, 24/7 nursing, in-room dining, cafeteria, parking, and rehabilitation services for a comfortable stay.

What amenities are provided for international patients?

International patients receive visa assistance, airport transfers, a dedicated lounge, case management, FRRO help, currency exchange, and concierge services.

What are the visiting hours?

Visiting hours vary; please contact 022 6767 0101 for current timings and visitor guidelines.

How to cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Call the appointment desk or use the booking platform to cancel or reschedule. Early notice is appreciated to accommodate others.
