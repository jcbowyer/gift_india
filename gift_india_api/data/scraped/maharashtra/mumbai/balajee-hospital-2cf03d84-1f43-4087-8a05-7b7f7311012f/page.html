SBS group has started its hospital industry journey with a 10 bedded hospital in mumbai 12 years ago with high ambition and a strong purpose to serve people by the founder director Mr. Shashi Bhushan Sharma.

Now with his clear vision and sharp entrepreneurial skill SBS GROUP is managing 5 hospitals pan India having more than 450 beds and his vision is that SBS group shall be having of 20 hospitals by 2030 with total bed capacity of more than 2000.
