Fauziya Hospital is a very well-known hospital in the vicinity of Kurla West. Successfully established on 6th December, 1995, It is one of the well-known hospitals having 150 staff, 40 consulting doctors from different faculty. It has 62 beds, treats almost 4000 IPD patients and 36,000 OPD patients per year. Fauziya has an expert team of Consultants who are specifically trained in different subspecialties from some of the leading healthcare institute of India. It’s a multi-speciality hospital from different faculties like General Medicine, General Surgery, Gynaecology and Obstetrics, ENT, Orthopaedics, Ophthalmology. Provides 24 X 7 care to all the sections of the society at a very economical cost with all modern arts of technology.
