About Hospital

Welcome to MT Hospital

Maria Theresa Hospital was established as a movement of our beloved Foundress, Saint Mariam Thresia, to continue her healing mission with merciful love and care. The hospital comprises many departments and facilities, providing efficient medical care and gaining popularity over the past 47 years. We are accredited with NABH Entry-Level Accreditation and consistently prioritize patient safety as our primary concern.

Our hospital is a not-for-profit organization registered as a charitable trust. It is a 200-bedded multi-speciality hospital, fully equipped with operation theatres (OT), a post-surgical ICU, a medical ICU, and a NICU with ventilator support. We also provide training in various nursing courses.
