Welcome to Netrajyoti

Netrajyoti Eye Hospital is a dream comes true. It was a dream nurtured by a Retina fellow of Dr. S.S.Badrinath, founder & mentor of Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai.The dream had to be nurtured by the fellow in his mind for one and half decade to make it a reality . This fellow is none other than Dr. Pradip Mohanta. After 6 years of good private practice, following the footsteps of Dr. S.S.Badrinath, Dr. Pradip left his private practice and formed a public charitable trust in 2000 named Netrajyoti Eye Reserch Foundation. People encouraging, helping, co-operating and keeping the moral high in the mind for this sacrifice are Dr. Amitava Biswas, Mr. R.P. Mukherjee, Mrs. Dipali Mohanta, Mr. Uttam Mohanta, Mr. Sudip Biswas, Mr. Shubhendu Chakraborty, Mr. Anil Kr. Mohanta and Mrs. Renuka Mohanta.
