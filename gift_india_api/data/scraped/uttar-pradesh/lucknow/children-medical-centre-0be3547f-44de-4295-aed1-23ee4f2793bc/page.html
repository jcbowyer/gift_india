About CMC

Children Medical Centre

CHILDREN MEDICAL CENTRE houses the sub-specialities of Pediatric Surgery, Electrophysiology and Pacing, Clinical and Preventive Cardiology and Interventional Cardiology, where an integrated team of cardiac surgeons and cardiologists works in tandem. Backed up with the latest technology including a unique hybrid operating suite, the dedicated team of Cardiac Surgery doctors has performed a staggering 50,000+ CABG surgeries. The team is expert in off-pump CABG, robotic heart surgery, and minimally invasive valve and coronary heart bypass techniques.
