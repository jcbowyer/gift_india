Dr Manish Khanna

Dr. Khanna, Professor Orthopaedics is a Former Sr Consultant Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute Lucknow. Also, an Indian member of the International Clubfoot Study Group & has been awarded IOS – UK Fellowship 2009 from Kings college Hospital NHS, London. His area of expertise is Ankle & Foot surgeries – deformities correction in Clubfoot, Cerebral palsy, and Orthopaedic Rheumatology, especially in Regenerative Science.
