Our Services

We Provide Best Services

Services offered at Kamla Devi IVF Center include:

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

A advance technique for fertilizing eggs with sperm outside the body, offering hope to couples facing complex infertility issues.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

It offers a less invasive alternative to IVF and can be an effective option depending on individual circumstances. Involves placing sperm inside a woman's uterus.

Egg Donation and Surrogacy

Egg donation and surrogacy are two methods used in reproduction for individuals or couples who face challenges conceiving pregnancy.

Fertility Preservation

Techniques to preserve fertility for individuals facing medical treatments that may impact their reproductive capabilities.Eggs are retrieved from the ovaries, frozen, and stored for future use.
