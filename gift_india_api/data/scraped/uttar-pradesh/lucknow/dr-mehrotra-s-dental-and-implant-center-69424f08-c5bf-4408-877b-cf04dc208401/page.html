Located in the heart of Hazratganj, our practice was established in 1954 by Dr. Prakash Narayan Mehrotra. For over 60 years we have been providing cutting edge dental care for the city of nawabs. Currently owned and run by his son, Dr. Nikhil Mehrotra (MBBS, BDS, MDS-Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, Implantologist), our practice offers complete dental solutions for all age groups, with special emphasis on implant surgery and implant supported prosthetic tooth replacements.
