About Our Hospital

UHC is accredited by NABH Accredited, Manyata, Kayakalp, Jeevandhara, ROHINI, ISO(9001-2015).

The hospital is situated in Eldeco-I opposite A.W.H.O colony, near Bangla Bazaar which is approximately 7 kilometres from Chaudhary Charan Singh Airport and about 4 kilometres from Charbagh railway station. It is a multi-speciality facility built on 9000 square feet plot area and is a three-storied structure with the total floor area of 11,000 square feet. It is situated on 60 feet wide road and has a parking area for 15 to 20 vehicles.The reception area can accommodate 50 people at a time. The waiting area is fully air-conditioned.
The hospital follows the guidelines of Lucknow Development Authority, is duly registered with Lucknow Nagar Nigam, Chief Medical Officer-Lucknow, State Pollution Control Board PNDT, MTP act, Fire dept etc.
