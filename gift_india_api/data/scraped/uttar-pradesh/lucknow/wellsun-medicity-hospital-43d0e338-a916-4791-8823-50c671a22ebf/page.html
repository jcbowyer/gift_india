Wellsun Medicity at a Glance

Wellsun Medicity is a premier 200-bedded Super-specialty hospital that offers world-class healthcare services to patients from all walks of life. The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art modular operation theatres,interventional cathlabs,and intensive care units,along with range of in-patient rooms that cater to all budgets-from economy to luxury.It strives to provide accessible, accountable,and affordable healthcare services to all members of society.

The hospital is renowned for its exceptional services in Cardiac Sciences, Renal Sciences, and other specialties, all of which are staffed by highly experienced and trained medical professionals who are dedicated to provide round-the-clock care at an affordable cost. Wellsun Medicity takes pride in its capabilities in handling Interventional Cardiology and kidney transplant among other specialties.
