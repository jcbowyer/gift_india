Who we are

Welcome To
Precision Urology Hospital

Precision Urology Hospital is a tribute to the dream once lived by its chief mentor, Late Sri Swami Dayal Mishra, whose unwavering faith continues to guide our way even today. Precision Urology Hospital started its operations in April 2016 as the only single specialty hospital in Lucknow division dedicated to Urology and Nephrology with the minimally invasive armamentarium available. Now a 100-bedded hospital, PUH has continuously added the latest innovative technologies for the benefit of its patients. The vast experience of the doctors at PUH includes Robotics, Advanced Laparoscopy, Minimally invasive Endourology, Renal Transplantation and treatment of other urological diseases related to Uro-Oncology, Pediatric Urology, Andrology, Female Urology, Reconstructive Urology, among others. In 2020, PUH became a dedicated Kidney Transplant centre.

We work on the motto that ‘Every life deserves world-class care at affordable prices.’

  • Our Vision
    Our vision is to be the region's first choice for patients, physicians, academicians, and employees by demonstrating best practices in urological & nephrological clinical care and medical research.
  • Our Mission
    We are committed to providing world-class, affordable, and comprehensive urological and nephrological care. We follow ethical clinical standards with a focussed single-specialty approach to urology and nephrology with all sub-disciplines under one roof.

Areas of Excellence

Our areas of excellence give our patients the impetus to come to Precision Urology Hospital. We believe in delivering extraordinary results with exceptional doctors using new and innovative solutions to urology and nephrology problems. Patients’ satisfaction is our top most priority and in order to achieve that, we have broadly sub categorized urology/nephrology into sub specialty areas that are managed by experts in the field.

Urology Services

The Urology Department at Precision Urology Hospital manages the surgical treatment and care of patients with a wide range of urological ailments. Our high-volume subspecialty clinics are well-equipped to handle the entire spectrum of simple as well as challenging clinical cases for the treatment of bladder, prostate and kidney disorders. The team’s knowledge and vast experience is supported by state-of-the-art technology and “Patients First philosophy”, providing compassionate and responsive care to our patients. We have a dedicated Endourology clinic that deals with the latest minimally invasive modalities for treatment of stones in Genito-urinary tract, prostate enlargement and obstruction in the urinary pipe.

Nephrology Services

Nephrology is a subspeciality of internal medicine which deals with diseases of kidney and urinary tract. The main function of the kidney includes filtration of blood and removing waste products generated by the body that are excreted via urine. Other functions include regulation of electrolytes, fluid status, and blood pressure control. Kidney produces two important hormones, Erythropoietin and Vitamin D. Erythropoietin is essential for normal production of hemoglobin; Vitamin D maintains bone health.

Anaesthesiology Services

At Precision Urology Hospital, we are committed to keep our patients pain-free and comfortable during medical procedures. Our anesthesia team, headed by Dr. Vandana Mishra, helps us diligently in that cause since anesthesia temporarily blocks sensory/pain signals from nerves to the centres in the brain. The Critical Care Department has full time anesthetists who handle high dependency patients in medical and surgical ICU.

Advanced Diagnostic Services

Urologists now have many tools to help diagnose disease in our kidney, ureter, bladder and urethra. Precision Urology Hospital has separate Radiology and Pathology departments that aid the treating consultant to come to a precise diagnosis. Patients coming to the Outpatient department are directed to the Diagnostic suite after initial counselling. Once the diagnostic tests and imaging are carried out, the treating consultant reviews the results and then the final diagnosis is made. All the tests and imaging required to come to a specific diagnosis are made in a single OPD day visit to reduce the visit-to-diagnosis time. Generally, these diagnostic tests and imaging are detailed, often pain-free, and quick.

Centre for Robotic Surgery in Urology Speciality:

Robotic surgery, a recent addition to the existing laparoscopic surgeries, enhances the effectiveness of the operating surgeon. Robotic surgery with the da Vinci Surgical System was approved by the Food and Drug Administration in 2000. The technique has been rapidly adopted by hospitals in the United States and Europe. Its penetration in India is speedily increasing with many centres now having their own robot. Dr. Shashikant Mishra and his team are trained Robotic surgeons. In the past, Dr. Mishra has performed complex robotic procedures such as radical cystectomy and prostatectomy with ease. We do realize that the high cost of robotic procedures may render them untenable for some. However, with the greater flexibility and control that they provide, the surgeon is able to perform complex procedures with more precision. Thus, it is our endeavour to bring forward a robotic platform soon to cater to the needs of our patients.

Kidney Transplant Center

Precision Urology is the best hospital for kidney transplants in Lucknow, offering unmatched expertise, cutting-edge technology, and exceptional patient care. A plethora of kidney transplants with minimum waiting time are performed at the hospital- ABO blood group compatible kidney transplant, ABO incompatible kidney transplant (patients whose blood group does not match that of their donor), and re-transplants of patients who have had complications in their earlier transplant. A 24-hour dialysis facility is available in the hospital and dialysis is conducted by skilled and trained dialysis technicians under the supervision of experienced nephrologists. Additionally, PUH is equipped to handle cases of pre-emptive transplants for patients who have suddenly been diagnosed with failure of both kidneys. The hospital's state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical equipment contribute to the high success rate of kidney transplants. The meticulous pre-operative evaluations, thorough donor screening, and comprehensive post-operative care offered by the hospital's dedicated team ensure optimal outcomes for patients.

Commitment to Excellence

Patients who choose Precision Urology can be confident that they are receiving treatment from the best Urologists & Nephrologists in the field. The hospital's team of specialists comprises experienced urologists, nephrologists, surgeons, and support staff who work collaboratively to provide comprehensive and personalized care. Their expertise, combined with their dedication to ongoing professional development, ensures that patients receive the highest level of medical attention and the most effective treatment strategies.

Beyond the medical expertise and state-of-the-art facilities, Precision Urology understands the emotional impact that genitourinary ailments & cancers can have on individuals and their families. The hospital offers counseling and support services to address the psychological and emotional well-being of patients throughout their treatment journey. This holistic approach fosters a compassionate and nurturing environment where patients can feel heard, supported, and empowered.
