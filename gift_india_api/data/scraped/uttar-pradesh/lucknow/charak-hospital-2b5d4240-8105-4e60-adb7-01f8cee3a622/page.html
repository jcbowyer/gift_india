Common questions about Charak Hospital, Lucknow.

Charak Hospital is located at Hardoi Road, near Safaid Masjid, Dubagga, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh – 226003. It is accessible from the city centre, Chinhat, Indira Nagar, and the Lucknow–Sitapur highway.
Yes. Charak Hospital operates a round-the-clock Emergency & Trauma Care unit with ICU, ventilators, and a dedicated emergency team. Helpline: 0522-666-4444.
