LASIK

Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis, more commonly referred to as LASIK,
is a procedure that corrects your vision by reshaping your cornea with a laser.

Read More

CATARACT

Cataract is the term used to describe a clouding or fogging of the natural lens of your eye.

Read More

GLAUCOMA

Glaucoma is the second-leading cause of blindness in the world.
Glaucoma refers to a group of eye conditions that lead to damage to the optic nerve,
which transports visual information from the eye to the brain.

Read More

RETINA TREATMENT

Retina is the light sensitive lining in the back of your eye. It contains millions of special nerve cells that react to light.
These photoreceptors send electrical impulses to your optic nerve, which your brain converts into the images you see.
The retina is very essential for your vision and diseases and conditions affecting the retina can lead to permanent blindness.

Read More

OCULOPLASTY

The eyelids and structures around the eyes are critical for vision. Injuries, congenital defects,
aging changes and tumors can cause pain, eye damage, vision loss and disfigurement.

Read More

PEDIATRIC OPHTHALMOLOGY

Pediatric Ophthalmology is specialized eye care for children and adolescent eye diseases, including strabismus,
retinopathy of prematurity (ROP), congenital cataracts, nearsightedness / farsightedness and genetic eye diseases.

PATEL EYE CLINIC

Established in 2015 at Kandivali (East), Mumbai by Dr.Utsav Patel and Dr. Deepti Nanwani Patel, PATEL EYE CARE is dedicated to providing our patients with the latest, comprehensive and the finest in ophthalmology services. We deliver effective, integrated vision care specialty services from our state-of-the-art, patient-centered facility.

Our mission is to deliver innovative, high quality, customer driven solutions in ophthalmology in an accessible, supportive, caring, and friendly environment; resulting in superior outcomes and a high level of satisfaction for patients. We are renowned for our high medical and ethical standards, relaxed ambience as well as competent and compassionate professionals.
