Gunam Super Speciality Hospital – Hosur & Krishnagiri

Gunam Super Speciality Hospital is one of the best hospitals in Hosur and Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, providing reliable and high-quality healthcare for patients of all ages.

Our Hosur hospital has 220+ beds, offering specialised care in cardiology, oncology, orthopaedics, nephrology, gynaecology, and general surgery. The Krishnagiri hospital is a 50+ bed super-specialty centre with emergency services, ICUs, operating theatres, and advanced diagnostic facilities.

Trusted across Tamil Nadu, Gunam Hospital ensures safe, effective, and compassionate treatment for routine check-ups, specialised care, and complex surgeries for you and your family.
