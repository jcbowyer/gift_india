Two Decades of Excellence.

Two Premium Locations.

For over 14 years, Nanda Dental has been the trusted name for specialist dental care in South Delhi (Defence Colony) and Gurgaon. We are a dedicated Invisalign & Implant Centre led by Dr. Smriti Nanda (Platinum Elite Invisalign Provider) and Dr. Prashant Nanda (Implantologist), established in 2012.

Moving beyond traditional dentistry, we combine our clinical expertise with the world’s most advanced technology—including the iTero Lumina Scanner—to deliver precise, impression-free treatments. Whether you need Invisible Aligners, 3D-Guided Implants, or Porcelain Veneers, we design smiles that restore your confidence. Now expanding to our premium centre on Golf Course Road.
