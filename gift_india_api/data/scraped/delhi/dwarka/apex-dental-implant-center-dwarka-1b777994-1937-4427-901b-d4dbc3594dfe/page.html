What is a dental implant?

Dental implants are a type of prosthetic device used to replace missing teeth. They consist of a metal post, usually made from titanium, that is surgically implanted into the jawbone and acts as an anchor for replacement teeth. Once the implant has been placed, it can be used to secure single replacement teeth or multiple artificial replacements such as bridges or dentures. Dental implants provide a secure and comfortable foundation for replacement teeth that look, feel, and function just like natural teeth.

What is the dental bonding procedure?

Dental bonding is a technique that utilizes a composite resin to repair and reshape damaged or decayed teeth. This procedure is versatile and can be used to improve the appearance of teeth by removing stains, closing gaps, or correcting misshapen or crooked teeth. A cosmetic dentist will apply the bonding material to the affected tooth, shape it as desired, and then use a high-intensity light to harden the material. Following this, the new surface is polished to a smooth finish. Composite bonds have a lifespan of up to eleven years.

What is the dental bridges procedure?

A dental bridge is a type of dental restoration that replaces one or more missing teeth with a false tooth called a pontic. The pontic is attached to porcelain crowns that are bonded with resin to the teeth on either side of the gap. This procedure, called a fixed bridge, helps reduce the risk of gum disease and corrects some bite issues. Dental bridges can last a long time.

Are Implant Safe?

The Dental Implant procedure is safe and has been used globally for over 40 years. The possibility of treatment can only be confirmed after a thorough examination of the candidate. This evaluation process reduces the chances of failure and increases the success rate of the procedure. The jaw bone condition, including its length and density, is the most important factor in determining the success and safety of the procedure. The longer and denser the jaw bone, the higher the chances of a successful and safe dental implant procedure.
