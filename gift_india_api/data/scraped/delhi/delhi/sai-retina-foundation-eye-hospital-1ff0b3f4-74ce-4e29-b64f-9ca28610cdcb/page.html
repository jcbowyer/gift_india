ABOUT

Sai Retina Foundation
Eye Hospital

SRF Eye Hospital was founded in 2008 by Dr. Jayanto Shekhar Guha, a distinguished senior vitreo-retina surgeon with over 30 years of experience, to meet the growing need for specialized retinal care in Delhi.
Over the years, the SRF Eye Hospital has become a leading eye care center in Delhi, renowned for its expertise in treating complex vitreo-retinal disorders. Our comprehensive services include laser vision correction (LASIK), advanced cataract surgeries, and cutting-edge vitreo-retinal treatments.
At SRF Eye Hospital, we are committed to delivering the highest standard of care, focusing on patient-centered treatment. We strive to provide top-quality eye care solutions at affordable prices, ensuring our patients receive the best possible outcomes for their vision and eye health.

Our Initiatives

In response to the pressing need for accessible eye care solutions in India, the Sai Retina Foundation has launched impactful initiatives aimed at combating preventable blindness and visual impairments.
These initiatives are driven by our founder’s vision to make quality eye care available to all, especially in underserved communities. Through innovative programmes like the Diabetic Retinopathy Mitigation Project in East and Northeast Delhi, we provide free eye camps and comprehensive support, striving to restore vision and transform lives.

Our Initiatives

In response to the pressing need for accessible eye care solutions in India, the SRF Eye Hospital has launched impactful initiatives aimed at combating preventable blindness and visual impairments.
