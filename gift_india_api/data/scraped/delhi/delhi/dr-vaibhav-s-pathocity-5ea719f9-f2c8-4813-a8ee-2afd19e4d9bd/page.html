Pathocity Laboratory, managed by Dr Vaibhav, provides high quality, accurate and dependable diagnostic services through modern, fully automated technology and facilities. The advisory staff are experienced medical personnel from respected institutions such as AIIMS and MAMC to ensure all the tests performed will be conducted with maximum clinical experience and precision in mind. Our lab is accredited by NABL & ISO, which greatly demonstrates our commitment to quality, consistency and dependability through our commitment to continual improvement in our laboratory operations.

Our patient provide home collection service for Blood specimens in Delhi, Gurgaon, Faridabad and Noida; therefore, all of our patients have access to superior quality diagnostic tests. The experienced medical personnel employed by Pathocity Laboratory review and verify the accuracy/completeness of all reports prior to issuance to patients. Upon receipt of their laboratory report, Patients have access to free post-report consultation services which provide assistance in interpreting and understanding their laboratory reports in order for the Patients to be able to make an informed decision regarding their healthcare.

Pathocity Laboratory provides preventative health care through offering a wide range of comprehensive health check package options. Our aim is to provide an a smooth, open and patient centred experience to all patients, whilst assuring that every patient is entitled to receive a trustworthy diagnostic service. Our highly-skilled professionals are devoted to delivering a superior quality diagnostic service to our Patients through attention to detail, and a friendly and compassionate manner.
