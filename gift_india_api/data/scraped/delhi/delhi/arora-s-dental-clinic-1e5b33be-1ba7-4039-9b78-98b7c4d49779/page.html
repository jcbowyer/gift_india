Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

What are charges for Root Canal Treatment ?

Root canal treatment charges range between INR 5000-6000 depending on condition of tooth, skill of the dentist and technology used.

How much Dental Implant Treatment costs ?

Single tooth replacement by Dental Implant costs around INR 25,000-35,000 depending on the brand of Implant, surgical situation and skill of the Implant Surgeon.

What are charges for Teeth Capping?

A good Porcelain fused to metal Crown costs INR 5000-6000 where as a Full Ceramic Zirconia crown costs INR 10,000 -12000. A Prosthodontist is a dental specialist trained to do Teeth Capping Treatment.

How is Smile Makeover Treatment done ?

Smile Makover can be done by Cosmetic Dental Treatment procedures like Teeth Whitening for Yellow Teeth, Crowns for broken down severly decayed teeth, Teeth Gap Closures by direct and indirect Veneers, Braces Treatment for crooked malaligned teeth.

Is Teeth Cleaning harmful?

Not at all. Its a misconception. Plaque if not removed by tooth brushing converts to hard tarter and calculus deposits which weaken the gums bone and teeth as they have millions of bacteria. These deposits are removed by special Ultrasonic Scalers without damaging your tooth structure hence making your Gums and Teeth stronger.
