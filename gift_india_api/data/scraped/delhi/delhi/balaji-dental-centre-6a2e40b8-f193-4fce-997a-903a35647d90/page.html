Our Journey

Balaji Dental Centre (BDC) is a premier dental facility located in Paschim Vihar, a prime locality of New Delhi, and was established in the year 1996 by Dr. Rajeev Gupta with the sole intent of providing a modern, ideal, and comfortable dental care to its patients. In nearly 24 years of dedicated service, Balaji dental centre has thrived and witnessed exponential growth while making a mark for itself both nationally and internationally. From a humble beginning, Balaji Dental Centre has come a long way and today, it comprises a large group of dentists, specialists, technicians, and hygienists. One of the QCI Accredited dental facilities in India, BDC has added to its portfolio a host of services including

Milestones

BDC has been awarded ISO 9001:2008 Certification to acknowledge our strict adherence to international standards of hygiene, sterilisation, and attention to detail in addition to our focus on international-level protocols of patient safety and satisfaction. Over the years, as BDC has grown, we have brought on board various specialists, technicians, and support staff to build a professional team. They come from prominent institutions, both in India and abroad, and have the ability, proficiency, and experience to be well-known and respected in their specific fields.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Rajeev Gupta has spent 25 years researching and perfecting the best techniques for veneers, implants, crowns, and every aspect of cosmetic dentistry. He and his team of top specialists provide unparalleled skill and experience, patient-centric care, and beautiful results.

Anti-Aging Dentistry

Anti-Aging Dentistry is an innovative approach that can years off of a patient’s face. By treating stained, crooked, or worn-down teeth and a narrow smile with anti-aging techniques, truly transformational results can be achieved.

Smile Makeover

Most patients don’t realize that their dental reconstruction doesn’t have to take very long. BDC has the capability and technology to achieve incredible reconstruction work with 1-Week Smile Makeover.

Dental Implants

Whether you need replacement for a single tooth, multiple teeth, or a complete set of teeth, we offer the most advanced dental implants to effectively replace missing teeth and restore your smile, functionality, and self-confidence.

Dental Veneers

Dr. Rajeev Gupta has developed Infinity Veneers, a groundbreaking innovation that tackles the problems that traditional veneers have such as staining and transparency. Through many experiments, he designed superior veneers that avoid these issues.
