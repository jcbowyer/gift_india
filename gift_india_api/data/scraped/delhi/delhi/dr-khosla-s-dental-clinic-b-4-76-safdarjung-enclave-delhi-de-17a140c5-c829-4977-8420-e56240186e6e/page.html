5. How much does RCT cost in Delhi?

The cost of Root Canal Treatment (RCT) in Delhi typically ranges from ₹3,000 to ₹12,000 per tooth, depending on factors like the tooth’s condition, location, and materials used. Since the complications related to the RCT are subjective for every patient, it goes without saying that the cost of the treatment will be adjusted accordingly. At Dr. Khosla’s Clinic, you will get a thorough rundown of all the involved costs so you can make informed decisions.
