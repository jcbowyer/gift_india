Meet Your Doctor

Compassionate ortho care by Dr. Nargesh Agrawal

At Child Ortho Care, we are dedicated to specialised orthopedic treatment for infants, children, and adolescents. Founded and led by Dr. Nargesh Agrawal, a renowned pediatric orthopedic surgeon, our centre offers compassionate, advanced care for clubfoot, limb deformities, hip dysplasia, pediatric fractures, and neuromuscular disorders.

Whether you're seeking a second opinion, an early diagnosis, or specialised treatment for your child, we're with you every step of the way.

Why Families Choose Us

A clinic built around the child, not the chart

01

Pediatric-only Practice

Every surgical and non-surgical decision is tuned for growing bones, not adapted from adult orthopedics.

02

Conservative First

Most cases — clubfoot, knock knees, bowlegs, mild scoliosis — start with bracing, casting, or physiotherapy. Surgery is the last lever, not the first.

03

Family-friendly Workflow

Same-day diagnostics where possible. Clear paperwork. WhatsApp follow-ups. Reception that speaks Hindi and English.

04

International Patient Desk

Travel coordination, visa support, accommodation, airport pickup, and online follow-ups for families flying in.
