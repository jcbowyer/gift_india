Why Choose Us

Pushpanjali Medical Centre is a well-equipped multi-specialty hospital, which caters to all specialties and super specialties. PMC is a comprehensive secondary care facility with excellent round the clock back up of Emergency, Critical Care, lab, ambulance and pharmacy services.

Pushpanjali offers world class care with compassion. Care that is amply reflected to the world through the globally comparable Medicare facilities at Pushpanjali and compassion that's inherent in each and every consultant and staff member present there.
