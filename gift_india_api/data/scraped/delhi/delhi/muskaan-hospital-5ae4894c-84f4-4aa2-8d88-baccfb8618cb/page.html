Welcome To Muskan Hospital

Muskan Hospital & Research Centre is a collective effort with an aim to initiate a hospital-project with various facilities under one roof. It has been established with a vision to become a leader in the field of integrated health care. Inaugurated with 40 beds on 15th November 2005 it has become 120 beds hospital now, of which about 16 beds are dedicated to ICU Care & 20 beds for NICU Care.
Muskan Hospital is on panel of imminent Corporate for indoor treatment & medical services. We have designed package of corporate health services to ensure that employees of its corporate clients stay healthy and productive. The aim is to impart expert health advice and ensure that medical. Facilities Available

  • Dialysis & Blood Gas Analyzer, Hormone Analyzer (Chemiilluscence Technique, BECKMAN & COLTER) – First Time in Bokaro, in any Private Setup.
  • 120 Bedded multi-specialty hospital with 16 Bedded ICCU, Six Bedded HDU, 20 Bedded NICU, AC Rooms, Non AC Rooms & General Ward.
  • ICCU with Multi-para Monitors, Infusion pump on each bed, incorporated with Defibrillator & Ventilator with all new modes of CPAP & BIPAP and specially suited for Poisoning & Critically ill patients and Chemotherapy.
  • Centralized Oxygen Supply, Central Suction & Compressed Air to each bed.
  • State of the Art Operation Theater where all sorts of Laparoscopic Surgeries & TURP are done.
