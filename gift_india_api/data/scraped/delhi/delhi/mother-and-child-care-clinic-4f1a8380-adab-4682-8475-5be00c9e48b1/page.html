Symptoms of Gynecological Diseases You Should Know

Be aware of common symptoms associated with gynecological diseases. If you experience any of the following signs, it’s important to consult with your Ob-Gyn promptly:

Abnormal vaginal bleeding or discharge

Pain during intercourse

Persistent pelvic pain or discomfort

Unexplained weight loss or gain

Irregular menstrual cycles

Severe abdominal bloating
