About AASTHA HOSPITAL

Aastha Hospital is a state-of-the-art Multi Speciality facility with advance technology over 50 beds providing quality healthcare services in first phase. Hospital is the most promising facility to the community of surroundings since its inception. Modern infrastructure complying all statutory, safety guidelines and fulfilling quality standards as per NABH.

Aastha Hospital has started its journey in January, 2008 with an honest and a focused vision to meet the need of community of surroundings. In a very short span of time, the hospital has become the prime tertiary healthcare Centre and recognised as one of the best healthcare facility in the West region of Delhi.
