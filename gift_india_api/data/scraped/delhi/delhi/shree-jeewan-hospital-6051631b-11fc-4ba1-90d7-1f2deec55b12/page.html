Welcome to Shree Jeewan Hospital

Shree Jeewan Hospital is a Multispecialty Hospital, established in 1920, providing comprehensive medical care. We have well furnished three modern Operation Theatres & 24 hours clinical lab, X-ray, E.C.G. and ultrasound facility with 24 hours Emergency. Our treatment facilities with technologically advanced equipment and systems are all backed by the expertise and reputation of the Hospital's consultants, resident doctors, nurses and paramedics.
