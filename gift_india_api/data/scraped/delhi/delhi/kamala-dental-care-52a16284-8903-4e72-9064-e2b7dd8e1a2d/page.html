Meet Our Doctors

Dr. Kamala Kakumanu

Chief Dental Surgeon

Graduating with distinction from JSS Dental College in 1997, Dr. Kamala founded Kamala Dental Care with a passion to provide world-class treatment under one roof. She has advanced training in Lasers (Aachen University, Germany), Cosmetic Dentistry (SUNY, USA), Esthetic Dentistry (Istanbul), and Ceramic Restorations (Geneva). A Dawson Scholar and faculty member, she is recognized internationally for expertise in smile design, restorative, and complex dental care. With her team of specialists, she combines compassion, innovation, and precision to deliver personalized treatments and lasting results.
