FAQs

Affordable Pricing that Doesn't Sacrifice Quality

What services do you offer?

We provide a range of services including hearing assessments, speech therapy, cochlear implants, and hearing aid fittings.

How do I know if I need a hearing aid?

If you're experiencing difficulty hearing conversations, especially in noisy environments, or find yourself asking people to repeat themselves often, it might be time to schedule a hearing test.

Are your clinicians certified?

Yes, all our audiologists and speech therapists are certified and have extensive training in their fields.

What types of hearing aids do you offer?

We offer a variety of hearing aids including behind-the-ear, in-the-ear, and completely-in-canal models from leading manufacturers.

Can children receive services at your clinic?

Absolutely, we offer speech and hearing services for clients of all ages, including children.

How long does a typical speech therapy session last?

Each session typically lasts about 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the specific needs of the client.

What is the process for getting a cochlear implant?

The process involves a comprehensive evaluation to determine candidacy, followed by the surgical procedure and subsequent rehabilitation sessions.

Do you offer custom treatment plans?

Yes, we customize treatment plans based on individual assessments to meet the unique needs of each client.

How can I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment by calling our clinic directly or booking through our website.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us with your insurance details so we can confirm coverage.
