About Batra Hospital

Patient centricity and best in class services is what drives Batra Hospital. Know us to know healthcare at its best.

Batra Hospital & Medical Research Centre was established in 1987 by Ch. Aishi Ram Batra Public Charitable Trust with an infrastructure comprising 500 beds, 10 Operation Theatres, 80 ICU beds, 24x7 Emergency facilities, a full range of state-of-the-art diagnostic laboratories and comprehensive rehabilitation facilities. Batra Hospital is the capital’s first multi-speciality private hospital of Delhi. Our aim has always been to bring world-class medical care within reach of the common man. Over the years, it has progressed, bringing in the latest and the most modern medical care in critical and life-threatening areas like Cancer, Cardiac, Orthopaedics, Nephrology and Neuro Surgery.
