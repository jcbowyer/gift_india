About Triton Hospital

Triton Hospital is set up as a twin tower hospital comprising of 8 floors over a carpet area of approx of 24000 sqft with a potential bed strength of upto 80 beds.

It is an endeavor to make the maternity, neo-paediatric intensive care services and superspecialty surgery services available not only for the Indian affluent and international patients but also for the not so affordable families.

ln the first phase, the hospital has opened up to the people of Delhi, its OPD and IPD services with 50 beds along with a 12 bedded level Ill NICU, 4 bedded post op and paediatric ICU, 2 Modular operation theatres with C Arm machine and laparoscopic equipment, labour cum delivery room, 24x7 pharmacy, pathology, radiology and other diagnostic services. All hospital beds(ICU and non ICU) have centralized oxygen and vacuum points.
