frequently asked questions

How frequently should I go to a dermatologist?
Ideally, each person should visit a dermatologist once a year, or if you have skin conditions that require regular checks, you should plan to visit.
What are the conventional services of a dermatologist?
Dermatologists are physicians who assist patients in diagnosing and treating all types of skin problems. At Monaris Skin Clinic, our dermatologists can provide you with a consultation where you can learn more about your skin conditions and discuss the most suitable treatment.
What is skin rejuvenation, and what does it do?
Cosmetic treatments of rejuvenating the skin work towards restoring the beauty of your skin. They reduce dullness, fine lines, sun spots, and skin coloration. Dermatologists can prescribe chemical peeling, laser treatment, or advanced facials as per your needs to have your skin look healthier and younger.
Are anti-aging procedures safe?
The contemporary anti-aging procedures are safe in the hands of experienced dermatology experts. The FDA-approved options, such as Anti Aging Injections, dermal fillers, micro needling, and laser treatments, are customized to match your skin type. With these procedures, wrinkles can be reduced, elasticity can be improved, and your skin can achieve a youthful appearance with minimal downtime.
