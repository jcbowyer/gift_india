    ABOUT

    At DNS Dental Care, we are dedicated to provide exceptional dental care with a warm and friendly approach. Our mission is to create and maintain healthy, beautiful smiles that will last a lifetime. We believe that oral health is crucial to overall well-being, and we are committed to helping our patients achieve and maintain optimal oral hygiene.

    Our team of highly skilled and compassionate dental professionals is led by Dr. Swati Khurana Saxena, a reputable and experienced dentist with a passion for delivering top-notch dental services.     SERVICES

    A comprehensive range of dental services to address all of your oral health needs. Our experienced and caring team of dental professionals is committed to ensure your comfort and deliver high-quality treatments using state-of-the-art technology. Whether you require routine preventive care or more specialized treatments, we have the expertise to help you achieve a healthy and beautiful smile.

    Dental Check-ups and Cleanings

    Regular dental check-ups are essential for maintaining good oral health. During these visits, we will examine your teeth, gums, and mouth, looking for any signs of dental issues. Professional cleanings will remove plaque and tartar buildup, preventing cavities and gum disease.

    ​Cosmetic Dentistry

    Enhance the appearance of your smile with our cosmetic dental services. Options include teeth whitening to brighten your teeth, dental veneers to improve the shape and alignment of your teeth, and composite bonding to repair minor dental imperfections.

    Dental Implants

    Replace missing teeth with dental implants, which are durable and natural-looking artificial tooth roots that provide a strong foundation for replacement teeth. Dental implants offer several advantages over traditional tooth replacement options like bridges or dentures

    We are dedicated to work with you to create a personalized treatment plan that addresses your unique needs and helps you achieve optimal Oral Health.
