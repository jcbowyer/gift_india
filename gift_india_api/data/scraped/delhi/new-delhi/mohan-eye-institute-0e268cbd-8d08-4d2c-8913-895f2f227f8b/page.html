ABOUT MOHAN EYE INSTITUTE!

Mohan eye hospital is a reputed and well established eye care center that holds great expertise in providing best in class, high-tech diagnostic, rehabilitative and therapeutic services of premium quality in almost every sub-specialty of ophthalmology. We aim to bring internationally approved, advanced services of eye care at your doorstep. Mohan eye care hospital is manned by highly skilled, qualified, motivated and competent medical, paramedical & support staff. Our staff is committed to offer comprehensive treatments, surgical and diagnostic services including entire array of ocular subspecialties equivalent to international standards. Every staff member and doctor over here is committed to provide personal attention as well as most advanced, latest medical care to each and every walk-in patient visiting our eye hospital.

Here at Mohan eye hospital, we offer quality of eye care that exceeds patient’s expectations. Besides, we ensure safety as well as health of each and every staff member associated with us. Our vision statement is to endeavor for highest level of professional and ethical excellence in providing ophthalmic care. We aim to adhere or stick to operational protocols so as to lessen or decrease errors and enhance patient’s safety. With our high-tech equipments, skilled professionals and patient- centric initiatives, we cultivate a very distinct, unique atmosphere of integrated workforce involvement maintaining higher values of excellence.

We aim to provide our patients safest and best eye care solutions in a friendly, warm manner and that too at rates which don not burden your budget. That is our main focus is only on comprehensive and compassionate surgical and medical eye care.

 

In a nutshell, we at Mohan eye hospital are dedicated in providing world class eye care services by continual improvements or enhancement of our processes, systems, technology & through recruitment of competent and dedicated professionals of progressive mindset.
