Education and Work Experience

Dr. Shivani Gupta received her DDS (Doctor of Dental Surgery) from University of Southern California (USC), Herman Ostrow School of Dentistry, Los Angeles, California, USA. Prior to this she received her BDS degree (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) from MAHE University, Manipal, India.
She has been practicing dentistry since 1997 and worked in some top hospitals in New Delhi before starting her own private practice. Dr. Shivani specializes in cosmetic & general dentistry and heads a team of visiting consultants specializing in each branch of dentistry. Together they offer a comprehensive dental treatment for the entire family. She is also a recommended dentist by the American Embassy in New Delhi (American Citizen Services).

Dental Clinic in New Delhi

“32 Smile Stone Dental Clinic” is one of the top dental clinics in New Delhi, India. We provide advanced dental treatments by experienced dentists and dental specialists using the latest materials and equipments at modest treatment costs.
