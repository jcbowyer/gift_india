Till date, Visual Aids Centre is the only exclusive Laser Vison Correction centre of India. We Are Here For You

We fully understand that opting for Laser vision correction is a life changing decision and it is quintessential for you to know about all the options you have and what you should expect in terms of outcomes. Please note that everyone is NOT a suitable candidate for these procedures and we need to pick and choose the right candidate on the basis of a scan to mitigate all potential risks and post-op complications. 15-20% of patients are politely refused by us due to either an underlying eye condition or unsuitable eye parameters.

Since we are the only dedicated laser vision correction eye centre of India, we offer all forms of laser vision correction procedures which may or may not be available elsewhere. As a gesture of goodwill, we offer all our patients a free of cost suitability scan and consultation to check for their eligibility for these procedures.
