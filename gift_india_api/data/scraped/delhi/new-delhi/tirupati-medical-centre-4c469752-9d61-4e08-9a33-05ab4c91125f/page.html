Welcome to Tirupati Medical Centre

The doctors and staff at Tirupati Medical Centre in New Delhi are  proud to offer the highest standard of patient-centred healthcare. At TMC we manage conditions requiring primary and secondary care and offer a wide variety of other medical, gynaecological, obstetric and surgical services including antenatal and postnatal care, adoloscent care, infertility diagnosis and treatment, post-menopausal care, minor and major surgeries, childhood vaccinations and well-person check-ups.
