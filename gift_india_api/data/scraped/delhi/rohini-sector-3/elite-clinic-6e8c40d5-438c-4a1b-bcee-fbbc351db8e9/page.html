AYURVEDIC

In Sanskrit, Ayurveda means “The Science of Life.” It is often called the “Mother of All Healing.” Ayurveda reminds us that health is the balanced and dynamic integration between our environment, body, mind, and spirit. Ayurveda describes three fundamental energies that govern our inner and outer environments which are known in Sanskrit as Vata (Wind), Pitta (Fire), and Kapha (Earth). Everyone inherits a unique mix of these three doshas. It’s believed that all the health issues of an individual are linked to the balance of these doshas.
