Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 01.What services does Save Sight Centre offer?

    We offer all eye care services under one roof. It includes:

    • Routine eye exams
    • Diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions such as glaucoma, cataract, uveitis, and macular degeneration
    • Glass Removal Refractive Surgeries are also available like SmartSurf, Femto, Trans PRK, Contoura Vision, SBK, ICL, IPCL, RLE, CLE, etc.
    • Pediatric eye care
    • Contact lens fitting
    • Treatment for diabetic eye problems and much more.

  • 02.How often should I get an eye exam?

    It’s recommended to have an eye exam every 6 Months- 1 year, especially after the age of 40 or if you have any existing eye conditions or a family history of eye disease. Children should have their first eye exam at 6 months, then at age 3, and again before starting school.

  • 03.What are the symptoms that require an eye exam?

    If you experience any of the following symptoms, we recommend scheduling an eye exam:

    • Blurred or double vision
    • Eye strain or discomfort
    • Difficulty reading or seeing at night
    • Frequent headaches
    • Sudden loss of vision or flashes of light
    • Red, swollen, or painful eyes

  • 04.Are your eye exams covered by insurance?

    Yes, we accept a variety of health insurance plans. You can check TPA and Govt Panels or contact us directly to check if your specific insurance provider is covered. We also offer affordable payment options for patients without insurance like EMI options.

  • 05.Why Save Sight Centre is considered as the best eye hospital in Delhi?

    Every patient looks for a place where his/her eye problems can be diagnosed and treated by an expert and experienced eye specialist. We at Save Sight Centre works for patient-centred approaches by using latest technology. Our eye care services have been satisfying more than 1.50 lacs patients for past 14 years. If you are in Delhi and NCR, you can just reach us for all eye care services under one roof at A-14, Adarsh Nagar (Opposite Adarsh Nagar Metro Station), Delhi-110033.

  • 06.Can children get eye exams and wear glasses?

    Yes, children should have regular eye exams to detect any vision problems early. Conditions like nearsightedness (myopia) or astigmatism are common in children. We offer pediatric eye exams and provide a wide range of stylish, durable glasses for kids.

  • 07. Can I get an eye exam if I don’t have any vision problems?

    Yes, routine eye exams are important even if you don't have noticeable vision problems. Many eye conditions, like glaucoma, can develop without obvious symptoms. Regular exams help detect these issues early and ensure the overall health of your eyes.

  • 08. How can I prevent eye problems as I get older?

    To maintain healthy eyes as you age, consider the following tips:

    • Protect your eyes from UV rays with sunglasses
    • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
    • Don't smoke
    • Manage chronic conditions like diabetes and hypertension
    • Get regular eye exams to catch any issues early

  • 09. What should I do if I have an eye emergency?

    If you experience an eye emergency, such as sudden vision loss, eye injury, or severe pain, seek medical attention immediately. On our working days and time you can call for urgent care or go to the nearest emergency ward. Quick action is important in preventing long-term damage.
