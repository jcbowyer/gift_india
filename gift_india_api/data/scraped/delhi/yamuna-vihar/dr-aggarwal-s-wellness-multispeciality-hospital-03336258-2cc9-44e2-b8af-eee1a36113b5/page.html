About Us

Dr. Aggarwal's Wellness Multispeciality Hospital is one of the best hospitals located in Delhi with advanced healthcare facilities. The hospital focuses on delivering various medical specialties that meet the healthcare needs of the population. Here one can get the best treatment of diseases related to the departments of Skin, Hair, and Nails (Dermatology), Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Diabetes & Medicine, Urology, Pediatrics, Orthopaedics, Cardiology, ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat), Physiotherapy, General surgery, and Anesthesia. The Hospital has the modern facilities of Digital X-Ray, Ultrasound, Fetal Echocardiogram (Fetal Echo), Doppler Study, ECG, NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit), C-Arm Machine (OT), Modular Operating Theatre, Emergency Ward, Anesthesia Workstation (OT), Micro & Pathology Lab, Isolation Room, Sample Collection and Pharmacy.
