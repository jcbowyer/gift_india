Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Ayurvedic treatments and approach

How is Ayurvedic treatment different from conventional medicine?
Ayurveda focuses on Ayurvedic Treatment that targets the root cause rather than just symptoms. While conventional medicine often manages conditions, our Ayurvedic approach aims to restore your body's natural healing capacity through personalized diet, lifestyle changes, herbal medicines, and specialized therapies like Panchakarma.
How long does it take to see results with Ayurvedic treatment?
Results vary based on the condition's severity and chronicity. For acute conditions, relief can begin within days to weeks through Ayurvedic treatment. Chronic conditions typically show improvement in 2–4 months with Ayurvedic therapies, with complete healing often achieved in 6–12 months. Our high success rate reflects consistent positive outcomes from Ayurvedic treatment.
Are there any side effects of Ayurvedic medicines?
Authentic Ayurvedic medicines, when prescribed by qualified practitioners, have minimal to no side effects. Our formulations are prepared using classical texts and quality-tested herbs. Any initial detox reactions are temporary and indicate the body's natural healing process beginning.
Can I continue my current medications while taking Ayurvedic treatment?
Yes, Ayurvedic treatment can safely complement conventional medications. We often integrate Ayurvedic therapies alongside your existing treatments, gradually reducing dependency on painkillers and other symptomatic medications as your Ayurvedic healing progresses. Always inform us about your current medications.
What conditions do you treat most successfully?
We specialize in chronic conditions where conventional medicine often struggles: disc bulge, cervical spondylosis, arthritis, migraines, paralysis recovery, and frozen shoulder. Our 15+ years of experience and 92% success rate demonstrate particular expertise in spine, joint, and neurological disorders.
