Welcome to ORTHOPLUSHOSPITAL

A Complete Multi Specialty Hospital

Orthoplus Hospital is a Multi Facility Hospital situated at rural belt of West Delhi, Started in the year 1995 ,It provides comprehensive health care with proper patient care at nominal cost, Hospitals is empanel with CGHS, DGHS, ECHS, MCD and many other Govt. Panels it caters to TPA panels of 42 insurance companies to provide cashless or reimbursement health service. 

New Delhi being the capital of India is very advanced in the field of medical services but there was no setup within the reach of lacs of rural villages and poor farmer in serious conditions and emergency and most of them use to succumb on way to those hospitals. Taking this fact into consideration Dr. S.K. Gaur had that invincible courage  to lay down the foundation of a hospital in the heart of such (rural ) place which is surrounded by approx 50 villages and catering non-stop service to approximately 6-7 lacs people.

EMERGENCY –24X7 Emergency department

An emergency department (ED), also known as an accident & emergency department (A&E), emergency room (ER), emergency ward (EW) or casualty department, is a medical treatment facility specializing in emergency medicine, the acute care of patients who present without prior appointment; either by their own means or by that of an ambulance. The emergency department is usually found in a hospital or other primary care center

OPD Service

OPD means an Out Patient Department of a hospital. It is the section of the hospital where patients are provided medical consultations and other allied services.
