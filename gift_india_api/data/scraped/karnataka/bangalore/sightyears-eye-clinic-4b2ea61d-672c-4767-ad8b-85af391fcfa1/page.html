Welcome to SightYears Eye Clinic


If you are a new visitor, we look forward to meeting you and helping you improve your ocular health.

If you are a current patient, thank you once again for allowing us the opportunity to care for your eyes.
If you are a new patient looking for one of the best eye doctors in one of the best eye clinics in Bangalore, India, you are in the right place.
SightYears Eye Clinic is a private ophthalmology clinic devoted to the innovative and integrative treatment of various eye problems and eye diseases. Dr Sanghamitra Burman, founder and chief consultant ophthalmic surgeon has over 20 years’ experience as an ophthalmologist and is a well-known cornea specialist, LASIK and cataract surgeon. She is not only a leading eye surgeon in Bangalore, but is also among a handful of cornea specialists with dual fellowships in complex corneal diseases and phaco-refractive surgery from the UK.
