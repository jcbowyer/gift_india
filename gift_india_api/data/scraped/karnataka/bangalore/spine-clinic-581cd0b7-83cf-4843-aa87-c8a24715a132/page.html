About Us

At Spire Clinic, we understand that orthopaedic pain and mobility challenges can profoundly impact every aspect of your life—from the simple joy of walking pain-free to pursuing the activities you love. At Spire Clinic we are globally trained orthopaedic surgeons, each with years of dedicated experience and a proven track record of successful outcomes across the complete spectrum of musculoskeletal care. From joint replacement surgeries—knee replacement, hip replacement, and shoulder replacement (including primary, complex, and revision procedures)—to sports injuries treated through advanced keyhole surgery (ACL reconstruction, PCL repair, meniscus repair, Bankart procedure, rotator cuff repair, ATFL reconstruction), fracture surgeries for simple and complex fractures including open and compound fractures, spine surgeries, paediatric orthopaedics and deformity corrections, and regenerative orthopaedics (PRP, GFC, BMAC)—we’re here to address every orthopaedic concern with expertise, precision, and genuine care.
What truly sets us apart is our unwavering belief that exceptional orthopaedic care extends far beyond the operating room. Our commitment to you doesn’t end when your surgery is complete—it continues throughout your entire recovery journey and beyond. We take the time to understand your unique situation, listen to your concerns, and develop personalised treatment plans that align with your individual goals and lifestyle. Whether you’re seeking relief from chronic joint pain, recovering from a sports injury, or looking for the best orthopaedic surgeon in Bangalore for your child’s condition, you’ll find compassionate, patient-centred care at Spire Clinic. Your healing, your comfort, and your return to the life you deserve—that’s our commitment, every step of the way
