About Us

Vivan Bone & Women Care.
Fellow ship in Spine Surgery
Fellow ship in minimal invasive gynaecology & infertility
The clinic has a state of the art USG-Scan machine & digital X-Ray facilities. It is also tied up with renowned lab facilities where it is a NABL approved centre.
There are facilities for minor procedure which can be done with local anaesthesia for both the specialities. The clinic is also equipped with NST Machine facilities for foetal monitoring. We as a organisation strive for best care for our patients and hence have tied to give all the outpatient facilities under one roof.
Come experience the treatment if you need any kind of Women/Bone issue you are suffering.
VIVAN BONE & WOMEN CARE was started in the year 2015 by renowned doctors Dr. Varun G.B.S. & Dr. Shwetha S Kamath. They started their medical studies at M S Ramaiah Medical College, Bangalore and went on to do their Masters in Orthopaedics and Obstetrics & Gyanecology respectively. The services provided by the vivan clinic are
Fracture Management, Arthritis Management, Osteoporosis Management, Spine Care, Orthopedic Trauma, Physiotherapy, Second Opinions, Splints & Appliances

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic service offers consultation, inpatient management and outpatient treatment for patients with disorders of the musculoskeletal system.

Obstetrics & Gynecology
