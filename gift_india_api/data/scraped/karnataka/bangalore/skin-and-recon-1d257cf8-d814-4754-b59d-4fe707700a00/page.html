FAQ

When should I consult a dermatologist in Bangalore?

Yes, if you have any medical or cosmetic skin-related issues, you should definitely see a skin specialist in Bangalore. After carefully evaluating and diagnosing your skin condition, a skin specialist will provide you with skincare advice, a deeper understanding of your skin issues, and recommendations for the finest skin treatments.

What advantages can seeing a skin specialist convey?

You can receive expert advise on basic skin care, and specific support from a skin professional in addressing any skin concerns when you visit a skin specialist. A skin specialist can diagnose skin cancer and other skin conditions early on and offer the best course of therapy.

Can general practitioners heal skin conditions?

You can get some treatment for your skin issues from general physicians. But when it comes to identifying and treating skin issues, they lack the expertise of a skin specialist. Our skin specialist in Bangalore, Dr. Shruthi Chikkaiah, is qualified to treat skin issues of any kind and severity.

What is the difference between cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgery?

Cosmetic surgery is performed to enhance appearance (e.g., rhinoplasty, breast augmentation), while reconstructive surgery is done to restore function or appearance after injury, surgery, or due to congenital conditions (e.g., cleft palate repair, burn scars).

How long do plastic surgery results last?

The results can be long-lasting, but it varies depending on the procedure and factors like aging, lifestyle, and genetics. Some procedures may require touch-ups over time.

How painful is plastic surgery?

Pain levels depend on the procedure. Most plastic surgeries are performed under anesthesia, so you won’t feel pain during the procedure. Post-operative pain is managed with prescribed medications, and discomfort typically subsides after a few days.

How do I prepare for plastic surgery?

Your surgeon will give you specific pre-surgery instructions, which may include stopping certain medications, avoiding smoking, and making arrangements for recovery.

What is the average recovery time after plastic surgery?

Recovery times vary depending on the procedure. Minor surgeries may require only a few days of downtime, while more invasive procedures may require several weeks for full recovery.

Will there be scars after plastic surgery?

Most surgeries will result in some scarring, though skilled surgeons try to minimize and hide scars in natural creases or less visible areas. Over time, scars usually fade.
