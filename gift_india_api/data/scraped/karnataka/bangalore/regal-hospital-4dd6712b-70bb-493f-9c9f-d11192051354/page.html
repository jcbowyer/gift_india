Regal Hospital - Bangalore’s Premier Multispecialty and Kidney Care Destination

Premier Kidney Care Facility in Bangalore!

Regal Hospital, a leading multispecialty healthcare institution in Bangalore, offers advanced kidney care with state-of-the-art facilities. Located on Thanisandra-Yelahanka Road, near the proposed Bharathiya City, the hospital features cutting-edge infrastructure designed to deliver exceptional medical care.

Committed to providing accessible and cost-effective healthcare, Regal Hospital specializes in a wide range of medical treatments, including kidney transplantation, kidney biopsy, dialysis, and surgeries, adhering to the highest standards of quality and safety. Our mission is to make high-quality medical care available to everyone while maintaining affordability.
