Tooth Whitening

Teeth tend to discolor with age or become yellowish as part of normal ageing  process or due to habbits such as drinking too much of tea, coffee, alcohol or smoking.

It takes just 60 minutes to give a fresh lease of life to those dull stained yellowish teeth by transforming them into dazzling brilliant white teeth. In today’s time our appearance is vital to our success and social acceptance and 60 minutes of bleaching can provide us with the solutions.

With todays materials the procedure is extremely safe with assured results and virtually no post bleaching blues like sensitivity. Whitening now is very cost effective and results also stay for around 2 years or more and maybe just a touch up is required.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are the most advanced form of fixed tooth replacement available today. They can be used for a variety of purposes like to replace a single tooth, Implant supported bridges (multiple teeth replacement without taking the support of adjoining teeth as in conventional bridge) or to stabilize a loose denture for efficient chewing with stability and support inside the mouth.

A typical Implant consists of a titanium screw resembling a tooth root with a roughened or smooth surface. These pure titanium implants are placed surgically into the bone and allowed to heal for a period orf 3-6 months so that they get firmly attached to the bone. Post integration of implants with the bone necessary prosthesis is fabricated to provide function and aesthetics to the patient.

How does teeth whitening work?

Teeth whitening systems usually use a hydrogen peroxide gel that is applied to your teeth. The ingredients work to get rid of the stains left on your teeth from various things.

Will my teeth whitening results last?

Teeth whitening is meant to be permanent and is said to last about two years. However, most patients opt to come back into the office periodically to ensure that their teeth stay pearly white. Remember, discoloration will occur again if you eat or drink certain things in excess. Smoking always adds to teeth discoloration.

Am I a good candidate for teeth whitening?

Almost anyone can have their teeth whitened! As long as you have proper oral health, there is nothing to stop you from getting teeth whitening. If you have discolored or stained teeth, you can usually depend on teeth whitening to enhance and brighten your smile. In some instances of extreme discoloration, you may require porcelain veneers to achieve a truly bright white smile.

What teeth whitening options are available?

There are many different types of teeth whitening systems used in Detroit. From whitening strips and toothpastes to at home teeth whitening kits that involve a whitening gel and trays, you have plenty of options! However, it is best to work with Dr. Talia to decide which type of teeth whitening will give you the best possible results.

Dental Implants

Choosing an implant dentist is an important decision for any patient. To ensure that the implant dentist you decide to work with is right for you, you will want to ask certain questions. It’s also important to ask questions not only about the dentist, but about dental implants as well. Below are some questions to consider:

How long will the process take?

Of course, you want to know how long it will take to have your dental implants put in place. Inquire about comprehensive examinations before the process even begins. It often takes a few visits to the dentist’s office to have everything in place. You want to ask about the time frame for scans and x-rays as well as the time needed to make the dental molds.

What type of implants do you use?

You want to find an implant dentist that uses top of the line dental implants.  The brand used should be of a reputable company that is consistent and has a track record of successful results.

At the Glamour Dental Care, we use Implant One. Implant-One implants feature an Implant-Abutment connection, which is straighter than any other screw-retained implant available in the US market.

What type of technology do you use to place the implants?

Technologies in the dental world continue to advance every year. It’s important that the implant dentist you choose is using top of the line technology. At our office, we use digital x-rays and guided implant technology. Using these new tools make the procedure much more tolerable. Ask what kinds of technology the implant dentist you’re considering will use to make your dental implant a success.

What is paediatric dentistry?

Paediatric dentistry is the field of dentistry which provides diagnosis and treatments for dental issues of children.
