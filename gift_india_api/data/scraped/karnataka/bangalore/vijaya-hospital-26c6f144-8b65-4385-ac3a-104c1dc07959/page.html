Centers of Excellence

Our Devotion shows in the comprehensive care we provide for our patients. All of us at Vijaya Hospital are committed to our mission: To Help You Live Your Healthiest Life.

Anaesthesiology

The Department of Anaesthesiology focuses on patient management, developing anaesthetic regimens, and administering anaesthetics to relieve patients' pain and distress.

Nephrology

The Nephrology department provides care to patients suffering from acute and chronic kidney diseases.

Radiology

The Department of Radiology provides MRI, PET CT, Ultrasound, Sonography, Colour Doppler, CT, Dexa, Mammography, intervention procedures, and X-Rays.
