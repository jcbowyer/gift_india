View More About Us

Maiya Hospital, established in 1979, was founded with the mission and vision of providing affordable and accessible patient care to the people of South Bangalore

Today, it stands as a trusted 100-bed multi-speciality facility offering comprehensive healthcare services. Our hospital also provides cashless insurance treatment, ensuring quality care is within reach for every patient.

Our Mission

We understand that accessing quality medical care can sometimes be challenging — especially for individuals with limited resources, mobility, or busy schedules.

At Maiya Hospital, we are committed to removing those barriers by providing affordable, compassionate, and world-class healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors.
