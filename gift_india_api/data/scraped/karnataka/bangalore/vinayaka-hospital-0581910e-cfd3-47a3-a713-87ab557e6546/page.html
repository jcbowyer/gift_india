About Vinayaka Hospital

A neighbourhood hospital with hospital-grade care.

Founded in 2002 by Dr. Jaya Subrahmanyam, Vinayaka Hospital began as a women's health practice and grew into a multi-speciality unit serving families across BTM, Bommanahalli and surrounding neighbourhoods. We focus on getting the small things right — a familiar face at the front desk, doctors who remember your history, and clear answers when you ask a question.

Read our full story

Personalised care

Every patient gets a treatment plan shaped around their history, lifestyle and goals — not a one-size template.

A team across specialties

Obstetricians, paediatricians, surgeons and physicians who actually talk to each other when your case needs it.

Evidence-based practice

Care that follows current Indian and international clinical guidelines — explained to you in plain language.

Why families choose us

Care that feels personal — and clinically sound.

Mother & baby first

From the first antenatal visit to neonatal care — a single coordinated team handles both ends of your delivery.

Senior consultants only

Every department is led by a consultant with 10+ years of clinical experience. You will not be passed between junior trainees.

Round-the-clock emergency

24×7 maternity and neonatal cover with an on-call obstetrician and paediatrician any night of the week.

Clean, safe environment

Infection-control protocols, sterilised OTs and a dedicated postnatal ward designed for rest and recovery.

Transparent packages

Antenatal, delivery and surgery packages are quoted up-front so there are no surprises at discharge.

Two decades of trust

Generations of families from south Bengaluru — and counting. We treat children of patients we delivered ourselves.
