Who We Are

Kanva Sri Sai Hospital is an NABH-accredited, 100-bedded multispeciality hospital located in Bangalore. With over 9 years of experience, our mission is to offer world-class healthcare that is accessible and patient-focused.

We leverage state-of-the-art medical technology and the clinical excellence of our experienced team to ensure the best possible care for every patient. Through our commitment to compassionate service and continuous quality improvement, we strive to give back to the community that has helped nurture us.
