How It Works

Pragnya Brain Mind Clinic offers Comprehensive Mental Health Care by reputed and experienced Neuro Psychiatrist, Clinical Psychologists and Counsellors. The set up & the location of the clinic has been attracting people who want a better quality of life and solutions with total confidentiality.

The clinic is located in a convenient and well-connected area with ample parking space making it easy to get in and out of the clinic. You will experience minimal waiting periods, comfortable seating, and convenient appointment-making system with exclusive privacy and confidentiality. This means that clients can discuss their issues in total confidence and without worry or any hesitation. Well planned time slots and fixed number of appointments in a day ensure complete individual attention to problems. Well trained support staff ensures there are no disturbances of any kind. Clients are given ample time and opportunity to discuss their problems in a calm and comfortable environment.

Clients get to choose their next appointment date and timing; they are even helped to decide what mode of treatment they are comfortable with. After detailed history, the Psychiatrist may ask for Psychometric Assessments to understand the problem in more depth. Later the treating team helps the client choose the right mode of treatment: with Medicines, Counselling and Psychotherapy, or a prudent combination of both. A well balanced treatment approach ensures speedy recovery and long lasting improvement without relapses. The doctors are committed to improve the quality of mental well being of anyone who visits Pragnya Brain Mind Clinic.

About Us

History of Clinic:

Dr. Usha Rao and Dr. Anand Rao, two competent and dedicated Clinical Psychologists had a vision of improving the holistic psychological health of people. This was based on a model of ‘Psychological Wellness’, rather than ‘Psychiatric Illness’. Thus was born ‘Rao’s Clinic’, which constantly kept pace with time and people’s Psychological Needs. Slowly gaining a strong reputation for being a successful Psycho Clinic & Resource Centre, many Corporates and schools got added to the long list of satisfied clientele.

Methods like psychological profiling helped choose the ‘best suited person for the particular job’, yielding higher productivity, better office relations and lower attrition. Teachers at schools were sensitized to issues like of Low Concentration, Learning Disabilities, Hyperactivity and Conduct Problems. Early recognition helps better treatment and lower family stress and irritation.

Pragnya Brain Mind Clinic evolved as a need to integrate the neurobiological attributes to the psychosocial approach already in place.

PSYCHOMETRY

A Psychometric assessment is done to evaluate mental health problems such as Anxiety Disorders, Depression, Schizophrenia, Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, Conduct Disorder, Bipolar Disorders and Eating Disorders.

We recognize the needs for assessment, selecting the right assessment instruments to cater to these needs. Our Psychometry lab is equipped with the scientifically well validated test materials, to assess various areas such as Diagnostic Evaluations, Neuro Psychological Evaluations, Screening for Evidence of Specific Learning Disabilities & Children with Sub Average Intelligence.

Children & Adolescents

Children today are faced with more challenges than ever before. On the one hand they have to excel in their academics, the standards of which keep rising, while on the other hand they have to keep pace with technology, while at the same time managing their friends, relationships and their personal lives. Some children are restless, hyperactive, have poor concentration, have conduct problems, bedwetting, talking or walking in sleep. There are many students who have a fear of speaking in public, and though they know many of the answers, remain silent for fear of being ridiculed.

Several students, unable to handle stress, have psychological problems related to the same. Some of these problems could be not paying attention in class, dropping academic grades, chronic headaches and backaches, frequent anger outbursts and irritability, feeling that they can’t go on, getting tired very easily, not being happy in what they do, not wanting to talk to people, poor quality of sleep, and even thoughts and acts of self-harm/ suicide. Some develop lying, stealing, use of cigarettes, alcohol and other drugs as "stress relief". These add to the problem further.

In this tough competitive world, it becomes imperative that we equip our children with the best psychological resources that take them forward towards their goal. These include Concentration enhancement, Memory retraining, Communication skills, Leadership skills, Anger Management, Personality development & Anxiety management.
