OUR INSTITUTION

  • We offer specialized education and training in the field of dentistry.
  • It is a higher education institution where students pursue a dental degree and gain the necessary knowledge and skills to become dentists.
  • Our College provides comprehensive dental programs that combine academic coursework with clinical training.
  • The programs cover various aspects of dentistry and opportunities for research, community outreach programs, and dental health campaigns.

VISION

  • To train a community of competent, duty conscious and dedicated Clinicians, Academicians and Researchers.
  • To restore and maintain the oral health, there by improving the health related quality of life of the people.

MISSION

  • To establish high standards in oral health science education in the country.
  • To impart learner centred oral health education.
  • To inculcate human values and a sense of service among the students.
  • To create a high-end research and development atmosphere promoting scholarly activities that benefit the profession and public.
  • To take oral health care to the door steps of the under privileged.
