    Vikram Hospital serves as one of the best heart hospitals in the country. We provide comprehensive heart care services delivering the best possible treatment using cutting-edge technology, innovative techniques and an expert cardiologist team.

    From diagnostic to surgical techniques and complex to minimally-invasive surgeries, we excel across the entire heart care spectrum making us one of the best heart surgery hospitals. To name a few procedures, we perform angioplasty and stenting,open heart surgery, valve replacement, pacemaker implantation, Ventricular Assistive Device surgery, and so on. With the usage of modern technology across diagnostic as well as surgical dimensions, we are able to deliver the best treatment possible to all our patients which is at par with the international standards.

    Vikram Hospital’s Department of Neurosciences combines the expertise of the best neurosurgery, neurology, neuro-oncology and interventional neuro-radiology specialists with state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and advanced surgical technology to offer the most effective treatment to our patients.

    With more than 100 years of combined experience, our expert's vast knowledge enables them to provide the simplest of solutions for highly complex neurological problems.

    The Gastroenterology department at Vikram is a one-stop destination for Gastro-Intestinal disorders, including surgeries for malignancies. Annually our centres perform 5000 minimally invasive endoscopies, colonoscopies, small bowel enteroscopies and endoscopic ultrasonography.

    To provide the most effective treatment for our patients problems we have top-line equipment that helps our specialists work easy and efficient. We have 24 hours ambulatory pH monitoring with 6 channel impendence monitoring, which is first of its kind in South India because of that we are the best gastroenterology hospital in Bangalore.

    Vikram Hospital now providing regular checkups to treat and prevent potential health problems through out the course of the pregnancy while promoting healthy lifestyles that benefit both mother and child. During checkups, pregnant women will receive medical information over maternal physiological changes in pregnancy, biological changes, and prenatal nutrition including prenatal vitamins.

    Recommendations on management and healthy lifestyle changes are also made during regular checkups. The availability of routine prenatal care has played a part in reducing maternal death rates and miscarriages as well as birth defects, low birth weight, neonatal infections and other preventable health problems.
