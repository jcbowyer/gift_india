Welcome to Sushrutha Ayurvedic Medical College and Hospital, Bengaluru

At Sushrutha Ayurvedic Medical College and Hospital, we are committed to blending ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern medical practices. For over 23 years, we have been dedicated to providing quality education, holistic healthcare, and healing therapies rooted in the rich tradition of Ayurveda.

Our institution aims to nurture and train the next generation of Ayurvedic practitioners and healthcare providers, while our hospital offers authentic Ayurvedic treatments to those seeking natural healing and wellness.

Mission:

To provide an integrated learning experience that combines theoretical knowledge with practical exposure, nurturing students into skilled and compassionate Ayurvedic professionals. We aim to offer healthcare that emphasizes prevention, balance, and wellness through traditional Ayurvedic principles.

Vision:

To become a leader in Ayurvedic education and healthcare by fostering excellence in teaching, research, and patient care. We envision creating a world where Ayurveda is recognized as an essential pillar of holistic well-being.
