Bethel Medical Services, established in 1997 by a group of medical professionals including doctors and paramedical staffs is an organization, which caters to all aspects of medical care, including emergency, preventive, curative and rehabilitative services. It consists of a qualified and eminent team co-ordinated by Prof. Pr. Dr. Sunny D Founder & President of Bethel Medical Mission Hospital & Group of Institutions.

The organization ensures complete well being of the population by giving importance to timely diagnosis and preventive health care in the form of regular medical camps and vaccinations.
