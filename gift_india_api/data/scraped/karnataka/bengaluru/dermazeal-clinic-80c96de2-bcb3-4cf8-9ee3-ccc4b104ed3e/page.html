Welcome to DermaZeal Clinic

DermaZeal Clinic is a state of the art futuristic Clinic which offers excellent solutions to all dermatological, cosmetological & hair related concerns. Situated along the 27th main road, Sector-1, HSR Layout, Bangalore, DermaZeal Clinic is spacious with modern infrastructure and hi-tech facilities. At DermaZeal Clinic we utilize the most current, safe and effective treatment options available, including numerous advanced laser treatment systems that work to achieve the specific desired results. Our Team of tactful and skilled dermatologists provide excellent personal care towards every patient to cater accordingly to their dermatological, cosmetological and hair related concerns.

Our Services

Medical Dermatology



Medical Dermatology is the practice of diagnosing and treating conditions of skin, hair and nails. At DermaZeal Clinic, our dermatologists couple their broad knowledge base along with their adequate time and care towards each patient to provide the most appropriate line of treatment.

Cosmetic Dermatology



Cosmetic Dermatology focuses on the improvement and enhancement of skin and hair to keep oneself looking young and beautiful. Treatments such as acne scar treatment, photo facial, laser hair removal aim at refreshing and rejuvenating skin and reducing imperfections .
