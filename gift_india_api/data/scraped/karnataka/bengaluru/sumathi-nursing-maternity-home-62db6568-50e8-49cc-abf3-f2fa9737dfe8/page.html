Message from Founders' Desk

It was an epiphanic moment to hold this Bold Vision, a vision that took years of enormous patience and relentless hard work.  “Expertise becomes obsolete without humility and curiosity” – Adam Grant. Being assiduous, humble, kind, resilient through the hardest of times and thirst for knowledge is the drive behind what WE are today.  It led us to make this huge dream possible. From being born and brought up in a small village, practicing in remote part of Karnataka, to making an incredible ambition come true isn’t a small feat! The values and experience WE learnt through the struggles in this journey are the values incorporated in building Sumathi Nursing & Maternity Home (SNMH).
Compassion, Care and Kindness has been our Tagline for 40 years.  Every staff member abides by these values.
We see to it that every patient entering our premises receive utmost care and attention. Patient is our foremost priority and commitment. We will do everything in our capacity to fulfill this commitment!! Humane approach with highest ethical standards and reasonable prices is our promise to OUR patients. To see them walk out of our hospital healed, happy, blessing us is our MOTTO.
SNMH has evolved into a Mini Multispecialty Centre, keeping itself abreast to the current technology.  General Medicine, Maternity Cases, Pediatrics, General Surgery, Pediatric Surgeries, Orthopedics, Urology, ENT, Dermatology being our forte, WE have now further expanded our expertise to Superspecialists – Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeries like Bariatric Surgeries, Level -1 NICU and Step-down ICU. We have a team of renowned doctors, expert in their fields, and a team of well-trained nurses, so that our patients receive the most convincing and best care and treatment.  We have left no stone unturned, no obstacle left unchallenged in the pursuit of modernization, embodying latest equipment and technology at affordable costs.

The bright future image that WE conceive of SNMH is an all-round growth and to provide best and most comprehensive health care to our society.
