    Orthopaedics

    Orthopedics deals with the management of aliments of the musculoskeletal system. The musculoskeletal system comprises the body’s structure and the mechanics that make it work.

    Doctors in this field specialize in diagnosing, treating, and treating conditions affecting the bones, joints, muscles, tendons, ligaments, cartilage, and nerves in patients of all ages, from newborn babies to the elderly.

    At Navachethana Hospital, our excellent team of orthopedic surgeons is adept at confronting the toughest disorders and conditions related to bones and the spine. They provide the right approach to expedite your swift recovery, ensuring that you regain to move freely at the earliest.



    Orthopaedics Facilities and Services:

    - Trauma, fracture management and surgery.
    - Joint replacement surgeries including Total hip,, Total knee, Total shoulder and total elbow arthtoplasty.
    - Sports injuries including arthroscopic ligament reconstruction of knee, ankle, shoulder, shoulder stabilization and rotator cuff repair.
    - Joint preserving surgeries such as osteotomies and realignment, biologics and cartilage repair.
    - Spine surgery such as discectomy, stabilization and deformity correction.

    Frequently Asked Questions - Global patients

    • What are the modes of payment?

      We accept various payment method. At the time of admission payments can be made through credit /debit cards/ cash. Payment can also be transferred to Hospital Account through wire transfer.

    • What medical specialties we offer for international patients?

      We offer a wide range of medical specialties and advanced treatment. Please refer to services section on the website for detailed information.

    • Can I get Medical VISA assistance?

      Depending on the country of origin and duration of stay, you may require medical visa. Our team can assist you the visa application process by providing necessary documents.

    • Does Navachethana Hospital assist for travel and accommodation?

      Yes, we offer assistance with travel arrangements, including flight booking, transportation to and from the hospital. We also recommend nearby accommodations and also assist with reservation to make your stay comfortable.

    • Does hospital arrange for Airport pick up?

      Yes, Our International Patient service team will share the name and contact details of the person who is designated to pick up from the airport.

    • How can I stay connected with my family during my treatment?

      We understand the importance of emotional support. Internet access is provide for all the international patients to stay connected with your loved ones and family members.

    • What happens after discharge/treatment?

      Your well- being is our paramount concern, we offer telemedicine consultations post treatment and discharge. Our team will provide continuous support to monitor your recovery.

    • Any testimonials available from international patients who have received treatment?

      Yes, we feature success stories and testimonial from international patients who have experienced successful treatment at our hospital.
