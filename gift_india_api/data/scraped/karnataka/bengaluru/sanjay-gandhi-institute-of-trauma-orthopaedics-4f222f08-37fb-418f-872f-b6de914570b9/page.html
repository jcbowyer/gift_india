About

Sanjay Gandhi Institute of Trauma and Orthopaedics Partners with Manipal Signa Health Insurance.
We are pleased to announce that Sanjay Gandhi Institute of Trauma and Orthopaedics has tied up with Manipal Signa Health Insurance to provide comprehensive medical coverage to our patients, especially for those who have less than 2 lakhs package.

This partnership aims to make quality orthopaedic healthcare more accessible and affordable for individuals and families. With Manipal Signa Health Insurance, our patients can enjoy cashless treatment, hassle-free claims, and a wide range of orthopaedic services.
