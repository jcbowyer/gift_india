WELCOME TO

SPANDANA HOSPITALS

Spandana Institute of Mental Health and Neurological Sciences (SIMHANS) has been in the service of rehabilitating addicts and mentally ill patients for 15 years now. Over the years, it has successfully treated more than 3000 patients and is currently home to 75 patients who are in the various stages of rehabilitation. Building on a legacy of more than 40 years of offering affordable health care, Spandana Multi-speciality Hospital is the latest venture looking to serve the people of the locality. The hospital comes equipped with the latest and advanced equipment along with doctors trained in their respective fields both in India and abroad.
