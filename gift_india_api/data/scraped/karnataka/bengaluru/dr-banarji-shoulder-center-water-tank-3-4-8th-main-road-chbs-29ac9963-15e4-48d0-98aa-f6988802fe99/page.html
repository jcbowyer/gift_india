Shoulder Arthroscopy

Shoulder Arthroscopy surgery is a minimally invasive surgery that helps you get rid of many shoulder problems like rotator cuff tears, frozen shoulder, and shoulder impingement. This surgery is performed using general anesthesia that will put the patient to sleep for some hours. It only requires small incisions that are about the size of a keyhole. 

  • Shoulder Arthroscopy for Rotator Cuff Surgery
  • Shoulder Arthroscopy for Frozen Shoulder
  • Shoulder Arthroscopy for Shoulder Impingement

Elbow Arthroscopy

The surgeon performs it with minimal surgical instruments and minimal incisions to treat painful tissues inside or around your elbow. First off, general anaesthesia is given to put the patient to sleep for a limited time period. Then, an arthroscope (tiny camera) is inserted through small incisions to view the internal images on the computer screen. 

  • Elbow Arthroscopy for Tennis Elbow Release
  • Elbow Arthroscopy for Early Elbow Arthritis
  • Elbow Arthroscopy for Elbow Synovectomy

Wrist Arthroscopy

Wrist arthroscopy is a surgical procedure that uses a tiny camera measuring 1.9 mm to analyze the damages or defects inside or around wrists. Based on the internal images of the camera, Dr Banarji BH, inspects tissues including tendons, ligaments, bones, and cartilage.

  • Wrist Arthroscopy for TFCC Repair
  • Wrist Arthroscopy for Carpal Tunnel Release
